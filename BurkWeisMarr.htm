Marriage license for Joseph Burkhart and Catherine Weisend February 9, 1871 in Monroe County,Ohio.  Joseph, born April 26, 1850 in Monroe County, was the son of  Frederick J. Burkhart and Margaret Ottilla Burkhart.  Catherine Weisend, born October 10, 1847, was the daughter of Jacob Weisend and Catherine Ulrich.   Joseph and Catherine were the parents of eight children.
