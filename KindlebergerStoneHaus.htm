Directions to the Kindelberger Stone House

 

Directions to the Kindelberger Stone House from Clarington, Ohio:

 

Clarington, Ohio is located on Ohio Route # 7 at the intersection of Route # 7 and Route # 78.  Proceed northeast on Route # 7 (toward Wheeling, WV) for about 0.7 miles.  Turn left onto Route # 556 and drive about 5 miles to the intersection with German Ridge Road.  Look for an electric sub-station and the Bethel Cemetery at this intersection.  Turn right onto German Ridge Road (this road will take you to Routes # 25, 37, and 81 -- At least one Monroe County map also identifies the first half mile or so of this road as Boltz Rd.).  Proceed to the junction of County Roads # 25 and # 37.  The Kindelberger Stone House is the second house on the right after your turn off Route # 556.  The house number is 50037.

 

 

Directions to the Kindelberger Stone House from Beallsville, Ohio:

 

Beallsville, Ohio is located at the intersection of Routes # 145 and # 556.  From Beallsville, follow Route # 556 east for about 8 miles to the intersection with German Ridge Road.  Look for an electric sub-station and the Bethel Cemetery at this intersection.  Turn left onto German Ridge Road (this road will take you to Routes # 25, 37, and 81 -- At least one Monroe County map also identifies the first half mile or so of this road as Boltz Rd.).  Proceed to the junction of County Roads # 25 and # 37.  The Kindelberger Stone House is the second house on the right after your turn off Route # 556.  The house number is 50037.
