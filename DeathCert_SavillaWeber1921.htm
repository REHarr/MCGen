From the Death Certificate for Savilla Weber

#17176,* Summit Twp., Monroe Co., OH

 

Length of residence in city where death occurred: about 50 years

Married

Birth: May 28, 1849, Miltonsburg, Ohio

Age: 71 years 9 months 12 days

Household duties

Parents: Joseph Keylor** and Liddia Schell; birth place of parents, "Don't know"

Informant: Wm. F. Weber,*** Lewisville, Ohio

Date of death: Mar 10, 1921

Cause of death: Paralysis

Certifying physician: J. W. Weber,**** Lewisville, O, March 12, 1921

Burial: Buchannan Cemetery, March 13, 1921

 

Notes:

*The original certificate was marked RETURNED with the note, "Kindly give the kind and cause of paralysis." The amended certificate responded with: "General Paralysis caused by clot on the brain." It was signed by E.D. Keylor, Local Registrar, on June 28, 1921.

** It was difficult to read whether this was Keyler or Keylor.

*** The informant was her son William Franklin Weber.

**** The certifying physician could have been Jacob William Weber, the son of Philip Weber and Catharine "Cassie" Lemley.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
