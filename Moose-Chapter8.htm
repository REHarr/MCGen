Wood Family

 

Hannah Wood married Kent Mitchell about 1765. (See page 170 and 171). Hannah’s father was Isaac Wood and he married Elizabeth Wilborn. Isaac’s parents were Joshua Woods who married Mary Unknown abt. 1695 in Baltimore, MD164,165,167.  Joshua’s parents were William Wood and Mary Wicks165. Hannah Wood was Martha Hall Moose’s 2nd Great Grandmother.

 

Wilborn /Wellbourne/ Parkine166,167,168

 

According to several family trees on One World Tess, Ancestry.com, Isaac Wood, b. 18 Jan 1700 in Harford, MD, d. 23 Jun 1777 in Havre de Grace, Harford, MD,  married Elizabeth Wilborn, b. abt 1717 in Long Island, NY, d. 1783 in Harford, MD. The marriage date is said to be 1721 but this cannot be correct if Elizabeth’s birthdate is correct. Another site168, The Kings Meadow, states that Elizabeth’s mother was Elizabeth Mitchell and not Elizabeth Stookes as cited in several un-named trees on Ancestry.com. . Elizabeth Wilborn’s birth date is said to be abt 1700 in Maryland or Delaware. Elizabeth’s Ancestors are shown in the Family Tree Attachment.

 

Hamilton

Bowen/Moone/Wilsford/Boeshaar/Carter/Hymphrey

 

Margaret Bowen is listed as the wife of George Stephen Ullom in over 30 Family Trees on Ancestry.com. Margaret was said to be the daughter of Henry Bowen and Anna Moone and thus tied to a rich history covering the family names above and many more and going back to England in the 1500s. However, this Margaret Bowen does not appear to be the daughter of Henry and Anna because the ages are not compatible. She could be a grand-daughter but that has not be proven. So these families cannot be connected through this relationship
