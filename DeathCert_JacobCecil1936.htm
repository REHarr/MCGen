FROM  THE  DEATH  CERTIFICATE  FOR  JACOB  CECIL

Wayne Township, Monroe County, Ohio, #4998

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married

birth:  Nov. 10, Woodsfield, O.

age:  77 years 1 month 22 days

farmer

father:  Stephen Cecil, born Woodsfield, O. Rt.2

mother  maiden name:  "don't know," born "don't know"

informant:  Lydia Cecil,* Woodsfield, Rt. 2

burial:  Oak Lawn, Woodsfield, Ohio; Jan 5, 1936

funeral director:  L.J. Bauer, Woodsfield, O.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Jan. 2, 1936

I hereby certify that I attended deceased from Dec., 1935 to Jan. 2, 1936.  I last saw him alive on Dec. 31, 1935; death is said to have occurred on the date stated above at __:30 a.m.**

cause of death:  chr. myocarditis

                           bronchio pneumonia; onset 1 wk ago

certifying physician:  A.R. Burkhart, M.D.; 1-2,1936; Woodsfield

 

Submitter's Notes: 

          * Lydia Cecil was his second wife and she would not have met his parents.  Jacob and Lydia  did  not marry until 1930.

        ** This is either 2:30 a.m. or 5:30 a.m.
