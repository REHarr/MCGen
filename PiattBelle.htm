HENRY & MARTHA ARABELLA "BELLE" PIATT MCKELVEY

 

 

This photo of Henry and his wife Martha Arabella "Belle" Piatt McKelvey was estimated to be from about 1930.  It was likely taken at Belle and Henry's home in Salem Township on Sykes Ridge above Clarington.  The house and barn still stand in an S-curve not far from the eastern end of Sykes Ridge Road.

 

Martha Arabella "Belle" Piatt was the daughter of John P. Piatt and Elizabeth Shriver.  Belle was born Aug 1864  and died 5 Dec 1941. She is buried in Powhatan Cemetery, Belmont Co.

 

Belle was married on 5 April 1894, as the second wife of Henry McKelvey.  He was born July 1862 and died after his wife. 


Seven children were born to Henry and Belle.  They were: 

 

Ada L. McKelvey born 1890 (by first wife)

Floyd Cecil McKelvey born 1895

Laura O. McKelvey born 1896

Lester or Leslie D. McKelvey born 1898

Roy McKelvey born 1899

Ralph McKelvey born 1903

Ronald Piatte McKelvey born 1907.
