WAY SCHOOL HOUSE -- PHOTOGRAPHED IN AUGUST 2002

 

 

View of the Front of Way School House - August 2002 - Photo Way 1

 

Way School is one of the few one-room school houses that still exists in Monroe County. It was one of about 170 such schools that serviced the County in the early decades of the 20th century. It is located near the site of the previous small town of Way, Ohio. Way, Ohio has long since ceased to exist as a town. A single store (rebuilt in the mid-1990s) named the Whitacre Store marks the site where Way, Ohio once stood. The coordinates of the former Way, Ohio are Latitude: N 39 38.458, Longitude: W081 11.016. The coordinates of the Way School House are Latitude: N 39 38.479, Longitude: W081 11.124.
