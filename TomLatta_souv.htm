Thomas Leroy Latta (b. 18 Jul 1866; d. 3 May 1925) and his wife, Mary Adelia (Burris) Latta (b. 1873;m.1898; d. 1950-Cambridge, Ohio), were both teachers in the Monroe County, Ohio, School System.  Above is program from Haga School for the school year 1899-1900.
