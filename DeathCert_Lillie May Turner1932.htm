From the Death Certificate for Lillie May Turner

Woodsfield, Monroe County, Ohio  # 32266

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

female, white, widowed (wife of Howard Turner)

date of birth:  [no entry here]

age:  23 years 1 month 2 days

occupation:  house duties

birthplace:  Woodsfield, Ohio

father:  Warren Sparrow, born Illinois

mother:  Barbra Ricer, born Woodsfield, Ohio

informant:  Elsie Miller, Zanesville, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  May 21, 1932

physician's statement:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased on May 22, 1932,        that I didn't see her  alive on May 22, 1932, and that death occurred, on the date       stated above at 12: a.m.

cause of death:  Internal hemmorrage  [sic], having _____*  rupture parietal       pleura, rupture of liver,  rupture of spleen, dent in diaphram

operation:  no

autopsy:  yes

signed:  C. J. Fiest, Coroner; May 22, 1932; Clarington, Ohio

burial:  Woodsfield, Ohio; May 24, 1932

undertaker:  W.P. Galbraith, Woodsfield, Ohio

 

* Submitter's Note:  There is another word here that cannot be read.

 

Submitter's Note:  The above death certificate was marked RETURNED:  "Kindly have coroner state the manner in which the rupture liver, parietal pleura and spleen was received.  If traumatic, state the means and whether accidental."

 

The answer:

"The rupture of the liver, parietal pleura and spleen was traumatic.  The deceased was riding in an automobile driven by one Willis Burns who left the road, tore a tree out by the roots and threw this decezsed [sic] and her companion out killing both.  He is under charge of involuntary manslaughter and his case will come before the grand jury this month."

signed           ---------C.J. Fiest, Coroner of Monroe County, Ohio;  Sept. 13, 1932.
