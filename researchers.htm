RESEARCHERS NEEDED!

Our chapter is frequently asked to provide a list of people who will research in Monroe County. We are especially in great need of people who are willing to do courthouse research for those who are unable to visit Monroe County. If you are interested in doing this as a volunteer for the chapter, please contact us immediately. If you do this for others for a fee, please send us your name and address so we can provide it to researchers. This would be a tremendous service to researchers who are seeking their Monroe County roots.
