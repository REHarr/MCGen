OFFICERS of MCC of OGS

 

The Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society (MCC of OGS) has a current slate of hard working, capable officers. Under the guidance of the very energetic past-president, Karen Romick, the Chapter has made major strides in the past years.

Election of officers is held at the regular monthly meeting in November of each year. We are always in search of new blood to help keep the Chapter dynamic and progressive.
