The above are enlargements of the class that appears in Punkin Ridge School 1897 1898 picture. The picture was broken into two pieces. The upper picture (above) was the left end of the class. The lower picture was the remaining students in the class. Below, are three pictures that are further enlargements of people from the above two pictures.

 

 

This is an enlargement of Oliver Franklin Drum who is the student on the far left of the first picture above.

 

 

 

The adult in the above picture is the teacher, T. J. Straight. The girl directly in front of him is Etta Drum. The girl directly in front of Etta Drum is Josephine Drum

 

 

This fuzzy photo is of William Rosco Drum. He appears on the far right of the class in the second picture above.
