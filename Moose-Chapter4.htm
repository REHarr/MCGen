Sarah Ellen Earley was born on 8 Nov 1848 in Ohio and died on 28 March 1928 in Monroe County, Ohio. She was the wife of John J. Moose who has already been covered and she was the daughter of William Earley born on 20 Nov 1810 in Washington, PA and who died on 9 Nov 1886 and is buried in the Conner Ridge Cemetery in Wayne Township. Sarah’s mother was Sarah Benson* Conner born about 1821 in PA. They were said to have married on 29 Oct 1831 in Belmont County, Ohio135,137,156. The suggested birth date is obviously incorrect. From census records she would appear to have been born about 1813 or 14.

* Sarah and John J. Moose named one of their daughters, Laura Benson.
