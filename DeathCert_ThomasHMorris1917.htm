From the Death Certificate for Thomas H. Morris

#80019, Caldwell, Noble Co., OH

 

Married

Birth:  Nov. 3, 1841 in Ohio

Age:  76 years 1 month 17 days

Blacksmith

Parents:  Thomas B. Morris born in Md and Jane Mann born in Ohio

Informant:  Norval* Morris, Mt. Vernon, O

Date of Death:  Dec. 21, 1917

Cause of death:  Continued suffer with stomach & liver alements [sic] for over one year ___?___** carcinoma of liver and stomach over one year

Contributory:  carcinoma liver & stomach; duration 1 year, 18 days

Certifying physician:  John Finly,** Caldwell, O, Dec. 21, 1917

Burial:  Olive, 12/23/1917

 

Notes:

* The informant was his son Norval Taylor Morris.

** There is one word unreadable here.

*** Due to the handwriting the spelling here of Dr. Finly may not be accurate.
