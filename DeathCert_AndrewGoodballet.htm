From the death certificate for Andrew Goodballet

#14688, East Liverpool, Columbiana Co., OH    [marked RETURNED]*

address:  1302 Erie St.

 

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married

birth:  Apr. 19, 1860 in O.

age:  58 yrs. 10 mos. 27 ds.

father:  Conard** Goodballet, born Germany

mother:  Lidya Schell, born Pa.

informant:  Conard** Goodballet, East Liverpool

filed:  3-18, 1918; Alice Davidson, Registrar

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  March 16, 1918

I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from March 16, 1918, to -------------, that I last saw him alive on March 16, 1918, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 10 a.m.

cause of death:  Paralisis [sic]

signed:  S. W. Hemphill, M.D.; Mar. 16, 1918; address:  E. L.

burial:  Long Run; Mar. 19, 1918

undertaker:  C.N. Miller; address E. L., O.

 

RETURNED COPY *

DEATH---ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

State the cause and kind of paralysis:

          general shock

State occupation: 

          unknown

          "Died within 2 minutes after I saw him"---Dr. Hemphill

 

Submitter's Notes:

     * The original death certificate was marked returned and asked for additional information.

     ** In both places on this certificate the name is spelled CONARD although on other documentation about him it is spelled CONRAD.
