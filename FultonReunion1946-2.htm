The following note is from Kym Kittell.  I will leave it here temporarily so the rest of you can know where it comes from and decide if you agree.  Any of these names can be changed.  Please let me know what you think and add any names you can.  Lets try to put names on everyone in the picture.  Use the number on the persons body (or near their head if there is no body showing).

 

In photo 14, my mother, Marjorie Alberta Parks, is standing just behind the tallest of the little girls. Her head is turned to her right and she is talking with Julie's mom, Mary Jane Feldner. I believe the woman in the middle, back against the building in the polka dot dress is my aunt, Luella Elizabeth Huddle Parks, first wife of Howard Fulton Parks. I'm not sure, but that might be Howard's head further right in the very back peeking through the older ladies in front. The blurry man on the right edge of the photo looks a bit like my uncle, Robert Charles Parks, but that is a guess. The woman to my mother's left, might be Rolande Cardinal Parks, Robert's wife. Or that might be Robert standing to Rolande's right, barely visible. Comparing with #11, I think the elderly woman with her hand touching the littlest girl might be my grandmother, Mary Jane Fulton Parks. She is in #13 too. What do the rest of you think?
