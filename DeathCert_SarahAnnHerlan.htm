From the Death Certificate for Saran Ann Herlan:

 

Sarah Ann Herlan

Death certificate  #27444 was  issued in Noble County, Ohio.  Saran Ann Herlan, married, was born November 20, 1844.  She lived 71 years, 4 months, and 18 days.  She was a housewife.  She was born in Noble Co., Ohio, the daughter of John Kent Sr. from Pa. and Elizabeth Danford from Noble Co., Ohio.  G. H. Herlan [her son George "Homer" Herlan] of Summerfield was the informant.  [Her son was also part of the Meek & Herlan undertakers of Summerfield that are listed on this certificate.]  Sarah Ann  died  in the Village of Summerfield on Apr. 7, 1916, from chronic intestinal nephritis; duration, one year.  The contributory cause was listed as arterio sclerosis which she had for a year as well.  The certifying physician was Geo. L. Nemstein or Wemstein [sic?]  She  was buried April 9th, 1916 at Summerfield, ======= style="mso-spacerun: yes"> #27444 was issued in Noble County, Ohio.
