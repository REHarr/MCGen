Monroe County, Ohio Cemetery Inscriptions

 

Jackson Township - compiled by Opal Smith and Karen Romick (1988).  The first settlement in Monroe County was made in the this township.  This volume includes tombstone inscriptions from the cemeteries of Witten, Locust Grove, Gravel Hill, Frobish, Rowles, Griffon, Moszer, and Shook. All name index, 67 pages, $7.00

 

Ohio & Lee Township  compiled by Opal Smith and Karen Romick (1989).  Includes the cemeteries of Apostolic Christian, Duffy, Lude, Mount Union, Mount Vernon, St. Johns, Zion, Old Zion, Zion Memorial, Emma Grove, Harter, Old Hannibal, Boston Hill, Stetson in Ohio Township and Sardis, Parker/Duvall, Salem, Mount Moriah, and Mount Olive in Lee Township. All name index, 137 pages, $11.00.

 

Perry Township  compiled by Ruth Dougherty Hogue (1991).  Includes cemeteries of Antioch, Barnard, Drum, Greenbrier, Haythorn, Kneubuhl, Lentz, Plainview, Unity Baptist, West Union.  Also has a history of Plainview, obituaries and other notes.  129 pages, all name index, $15.00.

 

Green Township -  compiled by Karen Romick  (2000).  Includes the cemeteries of Chapel Hill, Williams-Gaston, Fuchs, Kraft, Fletcher-Otts, Otts, Laings Prebyterian, Jones, Hickman, Wright, Price and Adamson.  Also has history of the township, Jones Bible Records, the story of Peter King Jr., and early records of Laings Presbyterian Church.  Indexed, 61 pages,  $8.00

 

Benton & Washington Townships  compiled by Karen Romick (2001).  Features the cemeteries of Benton Township: Brownsville United Presbyterian, Brownsville German Lutheran, Old Brownsville, Brownsville Methodist, Murphy, Poole, Sims, Ring, Jericho, Trail Run, St. Pauls.  Also featuring the cemeteries of Washington Township:  Merrill Ridge, Henthorn, Thompson-Beardmore, Scott, Dougherty-Daugherty, Mann, Lamping, Edington-Knowlton, Jackman, Low Gap, Old Graysville, Graysville Church of Christ, Pleasant Ridge, Smith, Rinard Mills.  Includes Carson Cemetery in Grandview Township, Washington County, Ohio.  148 pages, indexed, $16.00.

 

Wayne Township  compiled by Karen Romick (2002). Lists the tombstone inscriptions from the cemeteries of Barber Ridge, Christy-Sloan, Conner Ridge Methodist, New Conner Ridge, Hartshorn Ridge and Winland-Pryor. Also includes additional information such as obituaries and notes on selected burials. Indexed, 72 pages, $9.00.

 

Summit Township - St. Johns (Middle Church) German Evangelical Protestant Church Cemetery Records by Lena Kahrig Pettit. A brief history and the location of each monument on a graph complement Tombstone inscriptions of this cemetery.  Soft cover, indexed. $10.00
