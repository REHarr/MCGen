Major Contributors to the MCC of OGS Web Site

The Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society (MCC of OGS) web site is growing and becoming more and more valuable to researchers due to the increased amount of information available.  This information comes from our members and visitors who contribute information to the web site.  Without these contributions the site could not grow and become more useful.  We encourage and need your contributions.  Even the smallest bit of information or photo or reference may be an invaluable resource for some researcher.  Please submit your material to the webmaster at Richard Harrington.

Some members and visitors have made unusually large and/or significant contributions to the site.  This page of the web site is dedicated to recognizing these people and their contributions.  While many other members and visitors have made valuable contributions it is not always possible to recognize each one.  Where possible, recognition is given to the contributors of each item added to the site, provided the contributor agrees and gives their permission for such recognition.   The acknowledgements below are included with the knowledge and permission of the individuals.
