From the Death Certificate for William Morris

Liberty Township, Washington County, Ohio #22443

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white

birth:  Oct 24, 1861, Ohio

age:  47 years 5 months 20 days

marriage:  widower

occupation:  farmer

father:  Wilson Morris, born Ohio

mother:  Hanna Long, born Ohio

informant: J. H. Morris,*  Ho------d, Ohio**

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  April 14,1909

physician's statement:  . . .  I last saw him alive on April 14, 1909 . . . .

cause of death:  at drilling machine

signature of certifier:  William Shafer; April 14, 1909; Whipple, O.

burial:  Hopewell Cemetery; April 16, 1909

 

Submitter's Notes:

       * J. H. Morris may have been his brother Jacob Henry Morris.

       ** The handwriting here was difficult to read.
