The students in the above picture of the Benwood School class of 1898-1899 are not identified except for the woman in back row, third from the left, with the word Mom written above her head. Mom was Clara Drum Keylor, grandmother of the present owner of this picture, Tim Morton. Any assistance in identifying any of the other members of the picture would be greatly appreciated.
