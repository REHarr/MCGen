Volume 10 This is the tenth and last volume in this series comprised of genealogical columns written by Mrs. Fedorchak for the Spirit of Democracy newspaper from 1967 until her death in 1994. Cemetery, court of common pleas, marriage, land patents, death, wills, Civil War Discharges, church, items from newspapers are some of the records included in this volume. Families featured in this book include Haugh, Curtis, Davis, Dement, Schwallie-Ulrich, Edington-Edrington, Stille, Dye, Hall, Craig-Piatt, Yunkas, Cisne/Cessna, Kochert, Dicks, and more. Every name index lists thousands of other individuals. Paper. 330 pp. $20.00.Postage is $2.00 for the first item and $.75 for each additional item. Ohio residents add $1.50 sales tax.
