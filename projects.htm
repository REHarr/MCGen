PROJECTS of the MCC of OGS

 

Each year the Monroe County Chapter of Ohio Genealogical Society undertakes a number of projects for the purpose of expanding the resources available to members and visitors of the Chapter. These include projects such as collecting data from inaccessible resources to be available in tabular or other convenient forms. Other projects involve making a complete inventory of all Monroe County cemeteries. Still others include hard binding important documents and books. The fruits of these projects are freely available to our members and visitors.

The majority of the projects undertaken by the MCC of OGS are done by volunteers most of whom are Chapter members. There is a backlog of important projects that could be undertaken if only we had more volunteers to help. If you have an interest in taking the lead for, working on, or just assisting on any of these projects or if you have a project that you feel should be accomplished, please contact our MCC of OGS president to volunteer.
