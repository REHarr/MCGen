BUCHANAN  PRESBYTERIAN  CHURCH CEMETERY

 

 

Buchanan Presbyterian Church Cemetery - 2002

 

This picture of the Buchanan Presbyterian Church Cemetery was taken December 16, 2002.  The church and cemetery are located on Ohio Route # 78 between Woodsfield and Lewisville, Ohio about 2 miles east from Lewisville.  The cemetery for this church is across Rt. 78 from the Church.  For views of the church, click on View 1 and View 2.  For more information on the history of Buchanan Presbyterian Church, see the companion photo on this site taken in 1928.
