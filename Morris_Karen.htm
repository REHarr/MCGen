Richard MORRIS was born in 1778 in New Jersey.  He died on March 30, 1847 in Monroe County, OH.  He was buried in Harmony Cemetery, Beallsville, Monroe County, OH.   The following were heirs of Richard Morris: Sarah (and James Hendershot),  William Morris, Prudance (and Slater Rush), Frances and (Casper Hendershot), Phoebe and [Ephraim Jones). Although they appear to be children, there could be a missing generation. (See the 1830 census of Richard Morris- male between age of 20-30.  He's gone in 1840.  Could it be a son of Richard and father of these children or could they have been Richard's family from a second wife?)

 

Phoebe Morris Jones and child died in childbirth. She and husband Ephraim were involved in a land transaction with William and Hannah Myers, but Phoebe died before it was completed.  Since Phoebe had inherited the land, Ephraim was considered a "mere tenant."  The quit claim deed signed by Sarah and James Hendershot stated they had "inherited from the late Richard Morris."

 

Obadiah Truax was the administrator of Richard Morris' estate in 1847.

 

Richard Morris first appears in Monroe County tax records in 1824.
