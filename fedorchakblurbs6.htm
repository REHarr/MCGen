Family Research in Monroe County, Ohio

 

Catharine F. Fedorchak is recognized as the foremost genealogist in Monroe County, Ohio. The late Mrs. Fedorchak published a column in the Monroe County newspapers for over 30 years. This is a compilation of her genealogical columns plus information she received in response to her columns. Each book is overflowing with genealogies, letters, records, and much more! Each volume is about 300 pages and is indexed. Each volume sells for $20.00 (Click here for more information for ordering this volume)
