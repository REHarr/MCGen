From the Death Certificate for Helen E. Kurtzsman*

Sate of Ohio # 21442

Place of Death: Salem Township, Monroe County, Ohio

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PRATICULARS

female, white, single

birth: Mar 16, 1919, Monroe Co Ohio

age at death: 10 days

father: H.E. Kurtzsman,* born Monroe Co Ohio

mother: Alma Knowlton, born Tyler Co WVa

informant: H.E. Kurtzman,* Clarington, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: 3/16/1919

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from 3/24,1919, to 3/25, 1919; that I last saw her alive on 3/_** and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 5 a.m.

cause: impacting of the bowels ***

duration: 3 days

signature: J.E. Berry, M.D., 3/26,1919, Clarington, Ohio

burial: Clarington Cem, Mar 27, 1919

undertaker: Fiest & Spring, Clarington, O

 

Submitter's Notes:

* Whoever filled out the document, wrote it as KURTZSMAN in the two spots, but H.E. signed his name as KURTZMAN.

** This number is a writeover, but seems to be either 24 or 25.

*** This writing was difficult to read and may be in error.
