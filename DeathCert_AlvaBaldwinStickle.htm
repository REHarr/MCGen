From the death certificate for Alva Baldwin Stickle

Marlboro, Stark Co., OH #83025

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married, stone mason

birth: Sept. 30, 1848, Ohio

age at death: 70 yrs. 1 mos. 10 da.

father: Samuel Stickle, born Ohio

mother: Lucy Smith, born Ohio

informant: Chas. Stickle, Marlboro, O.

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: Nov. 18, 1918

statement: I hereby certify that I attended deceased from Oct. 19, 1918, to Nov. 10, 1918 and that death occurred, on the date above at 10 a.m.

cause: Chronic Bronchitis (non-tubercular); duration: 10 years

contributory: Cardiac-Vascular degeneration: duration: 2 years

signed: L. B. Santee,* M.D., Nov. 12, 1918, Louisville,** Ohio

burial: Marlboro, O., Nov. 12, 1918

undertaker: Cassady-Turner, Alliance

 

* Submitter's notes: The writing was difficult to read and this may not be correct.

** This LOUISVILLE is the one in Stark County, Ohio.
