From the Death Certificate for Emaline Schell:

 

Emaline Schell

Death certificate #38731 was issued in Malaga Township, Monroe County, Ohio. Emaline Schell, single, of Miltonsburg, Ohio, was born April 15, 1869, and she lived 62 years, 2 months, and 3 days. She was the daughter of George Schell and Margaret Thomas, both of Pennsylvania. The informant had very shaky handwriting and looks like J. and another initial, perhaps an S. [Note: It is not the same handwriting as the John H. Schell who signed her brother George's certificate.] Dr. H. P. Gillespie of Woodsfield indicated she died on June 28, 1931, due to "skull fracture, accidentally fell down cellar stairs." In the space: WAS ILLNESS OR INJURY IN ANY WAY RELATED TO OCCUPATION OF THE DECEASED? the doctor wrote yes, housework. She was buried in Miltonsburg, June 30, 1931.

 

[Submitter's Note: The date of birth given by J.S. on this certificate does not correspond with the year of her birth as indicated on the 1860, 1870, and 1880 censuses of Monroe County, Ohio. The only part of this certificate in the handwriting of J.S. Schell was that signature, and Malaga Ohio Box 50 (I think it is Box 50). All I can imagine is that when J.S. said she was born in 1859 the fifty could have sounded like a sixty to the transcriber. J.S. could be the Josephus S. Schell that was listed as her sibling in the 1870 census.]
