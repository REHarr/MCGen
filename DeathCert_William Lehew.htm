From the Death Certificate of William Lehew

Wetzel County, West Virginia, # 13746

place of death: New Martinsville, Wetzel County, Dist. No. 5261, West Virginia

PERSONAL

full name: William Lehew

male, white, married to Rebecca Mercer

birth: March 15, 1847 in W. Va.

age: 81 years 7 months 2 days

occupation: retired mail carrier

father: Wm. Lehew, born, W. Va.

maiden name of mother: Elizabeth Lehew, born W.Va.

informant: Mrs. Rebecca Lehew, New Martinsville

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: Oct. 17, 1928

physician's statement: I hereby certify that I attended deceased from Jan. 1, 1928 to Oct. 17, 1928, that I last saw him alive on Oct. 17, 1928, and that death occurred on date stated above, at 1 p.m.

cause of death: chronic myocarditis

contributory: bronchopneumonia

operation preceding death: no

autopsy: no

signature: John W. Pyles, M.D., New Martinsville

burial: Mercer Cemetery, Oct. 19, 1928

undertaker: W.E. Wharton, New Martinsville, W.Va.
