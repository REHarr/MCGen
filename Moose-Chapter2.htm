Then explaining how Joseph was born in Maryland is not that simple since John Senior was most likely born in PA. He would have married and then moved to Maryland for a short period of time before returning to PA. He would then need to move to Virginia for the birth of George and then back to PA for the birth of John Jr. and Jacob and then to Belmont County, Ohio. Perhaps the birth place information is not correct or possibly one or more of these people came from different families.

 

This work still leaves one uncertain about the relationship between George Moose and brothers, John and Jacob Moose. But still no information has been found to prove that George was or was not the son of John Sr. His close proximity with the other members of John Srs family certainly leads one to believe that they may be closely related.
