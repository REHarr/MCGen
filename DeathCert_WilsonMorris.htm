From the Death Certificate for Wilson Henry Morris:

 

Wilson Morris

Death Certificate #48172

date of death - July 1, 1921

Washington Twp., Monroe Co., OH

date of birth - March 20, 1827

lived 44 years 2 months 12 days

place of birth - Ohio

color - white; sex - male; occupation - farmer

parents' name - Henry Morris

The informant James Morris of Rinards Mills did not know the maiden name of his mother or the birthplace of either parent. [Wilson had a son named James.]

cause of death - acute gastritis with heart failure as a contributory cause

duration of illness - 10 days

Attending Physician - R. H. Latta M.D., Graysville

burial - Merril Ridge Cemetery, July 4, 1921

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
