Trudy Alice Jones nee Brookover

Source:  A dedication in “Truth in Love” that appears to be a church bulletin

West, Leslie West

Source: Unknown

         Leslie West, 19, son of Harry and Juanita Mercer West, formerly of Moundsville, but now of Amada, Ariz., drowned Saturday in California where he was serving in the U.S. Air Force. Survivors include a brother, Alfred, and his parents of Amada; his maternal grandmother Mrs. Pearl Mercer and several aunts and uncles in Moundsville. Services and interment Friday in Tucson.
