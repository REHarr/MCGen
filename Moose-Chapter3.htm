There are several family trees on ancestry.com which present a scenario covering the Moose Family that moved from Germany setting up in Pennsylvania and then splitting into three factions. One remained in Pennsylvania; one moved to North Carolina and one moved to Ohio. The Ohio Clan moved first to Belmont County and then to Monroe County. There is another group of Moose Families in Monroe County that I was unable to tie to the group that came from PA via Belmont County.

 

Gall David Mussgenug or Mussgnug was born in Solligen Germany  about 1596. He married Barbara about 1821 and Johann Nicholas Mussgenug  was born in 1640 in Solligen, Germany. Johann Nicholas I married Anna Elizabeth Repple on 13 Oct 1665 in Germany.

 

 Johann Nicholas I and Anna had a son Johann Nicholas II born on 1 April 1685. He married Anna Catherine Hess and they had a son Johann Jacob I born on 10 Feb 1718 in Solligen, Germany and died in Allentown, PA. He married Anna Margaret Fuger and they had five daughters and two sons. One son was Johann JacobII born about 1740 in Germany and who died 1823 in Gettysburg, PA. He married Elizabeth Hoffman on 25 Feb 1766. They had seven sons and one daughter. One of these sons was probably John Moose Sr. born 16 September 1778 in Lehigh County, Pennsylvania.
