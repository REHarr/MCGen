This is probably Morton Ridge School. Monroe Romick is standing in the back row on the left. The other two boys appear to be Clyde and Junior Stimpert. The girls are not identified. The photo was taken about 1925. If anyone can identify the girls or confirm the names of the Stimpert boys, please contact Karen Romick or the webmaster, Dick Harrington.
