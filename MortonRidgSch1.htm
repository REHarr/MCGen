The students and teacher of Morton Ridge School located near Salem Church on State Route 255 northwest of Sardis. From left to right: Back row: Junior Stimpert, Josephine Romick, Monroe Romick, teacher Mary Leudy Winkler, Clyde Stimpert. Front row: Bernard Yoss, Berdetta Romick, Kenneth Marty. Photo taken about 1930.
