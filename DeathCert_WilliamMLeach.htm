From the Death Certificate for William M. Leach

 

On his certificate of death, #4792, Monroe County, Ohio, William M.* Leach is listed as a widower, born January 28, 1866, living 66 years 11 months and 12 days, dying on January 9, 1933. He was a farmer born in Calais, Monroe County, Ohio, the son of Acel Leek and Harriet Leek, birthplaces of parents unknown. The informant was R. F. Hinton, [the husband of his daughter Mildred Pearl] of Jerusalem, Ohio. Paul R. Turner, the coroner, said he died of a heart attack at 4 a.m. He was buried in Calais Cemetery on January 11.

 

*Submitter s Note: Other research lists him as William Henry Leach.
