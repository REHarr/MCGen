BECOME A MEMBER of the MONROE COUNTY CHAPTER of OHIO GENEALOGICAL SOCIETY

 

Join us! Become a member of one of the best, most beneficial organizations for resources and help for researching your family tree. It is an opportunity to enjoy a good time with nice people and have fun while doing your research. Join now -- it is easy.

Membership in the Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society has many advantages to our local members as well as people living outside Monroe County. Among these is the opportunity to access a wealth of unique information and data available through MCC of OGS resources such as our library and privately held material of individual members (with their permission, of course). MCC of OGS has one of the best collections of Monroe County genealogy materials in Country.

The MCC of OGS newsletter, the Navigator, is much more than a newsletter. Every issue contains valuable genealogical data that would normally be available only through arduous research of resources that are frequently difficult to access. Much of this data are in the process of being converted to computer searchable media that will be available through the Chapter.

The Navigator is delivered to all members quarterly as part of the low, low, membership fee of $10 per year per person.

Monthly meetings are open to all members. Guest speakers at nearly every meeting provides an opportunity for members to get first-hand information that frequently is not available through any other source.

MCC of OGS members are given priority in obtaining materials such as publications distributed through the Chapter that may be in short supply.

But, perhaps most important, the MCC of OGS members are a group of nice friendly people who are ready and able to assist you in the search for your roots. They are experts on Monroe County and surrounding area genealogy. Where else can you get such warm, friendly, genealogical assistance for a whole year for less than the price of 3-gallons of milk?
