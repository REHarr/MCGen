Biographical Sketch of Catherine Lehew

 

Source: HISTORY OF TYLER CO, WV TO 1984, page 377

 

          In 1917, while living in Wetzel County, 'Teeny' Catherine Lehew married Berl Leek (1896-1964), the son of Elisha and Emma Morgan Leek. They lived in Wetzel County, where Berl worked for the B&O Railroad, until 1926 when they moved to Marshall County, where Berl found work with the Hanna Coal Company until 1929.  During the Depression, they moved back to the farm in Porters Falls.   They lived there until 1935, then moved back into Marshall County.  In the early 1930's, Berl went to work for the Powhatan Mining Company and worked until 1943.  In 1949, Berl and Catherine moved to a farm near Clarington, Ohio.  Berl died of leukemia in 1964, and Catherine died in 1977, while living with her daughter in Powhatan Point, Ohio.  Both are buried in the Powhatan Cemetery.

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
