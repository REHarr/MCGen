Star School – 1929

Star School Class of 1929:  (From left to right) Eugene Weber, Lucille Denbow, Eurydice Greenbank, Edith Young, Lloyd Weber, Edwin Feiber.  Eugene Weber and Lloyd Weber were brothers.  Their parents were John Henry and Jenny Wheeler Weber.  Roy Weber who appears in the Star School Class of 1924 picture is also a brother to Eugene and Lloyd Weber.
