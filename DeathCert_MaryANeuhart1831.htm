From the Death Certificate for Mary A. Neuhart

Woodsfield, Monroe Co., Ohio #47782

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

female, white, widdow [sic]

birth: June 9, 1831; Germany

age: 78 years 3 months 4 days

occupation: housewife

father: John Schaub, born Germany

mother: Mary A. Schaub, born Germany

informant: Chas. Neuhart; Woodsfield, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: Sept. 15, 1909*

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Aug. 12th, 1909, to Sept. 12th, 1909, that I last saw her alive on Sept. 13th, 1909, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 8 a.m.

cause of death: cerebral hemorrhage; duration, 30days

signed: J. R. Parry, M.D.; Sept. 15th, 1909; Woodfield O

burial: Woodsfield; Sept. 15, 1909

undertaker: A.J. Tubaugh; Woodsfield

 

*Submitter's Note: Although the death certificate as well as the Death CD#2 indicate she died September 15th, the Pioneer Church cemetery and the obituary information indicate September 13th. Also the death certificate indicates she was buried on the 15th which is highly unlikely if she died that same day.
