1. Isaac RUSH1 was born in 1785 in Pennsylvania.


BROWN CONNECTION:  11 Jul 1823 Joshua Brown of Little Britain, Lancaster County, PA, died at the age of 64.  He was the son of Joshua Brown and Hannah Gatchell.  In his will, he named as his beneficiaries, Sarah Brown, and "the children of Isaac Rush."  Joshua married Sarah Balderston Ely 25 Dec 1800 in Deer Creek Monthly Meeting, Maryland.  It was also in 1823 that the guardianship order of the children of Isaac was found in Monroe County, Ohio.  Researchers of the Brown family have cited no children for Joshua Brown, but there is definitely a connection between our Isaac and this Joshua Brown.

Elizabeth was Isaac's wife in the 1850 census, but some believe she is not the mother of his children.  If Elizabeth was Isaac's second wife, then he married her before 1830 as a female age 40-50 is in his household in that census.  Elizabeth and Isaac both apparently died before 1860 as they do not appear in the 1860 census.  Isaac and presumably Elizabeth are buried at Mt.
