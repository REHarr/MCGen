Decoration Day at Sardis, Ohio Decoration Day celebration was held by the church under the trees by the river. From Left to right: Carl Goddard, Carl Schoonover, Ernest Brady, Everett Dennis, Harry (?), Harry Hayes, George Schoonover. This photo belonged to Harry Hayes sister, Anna Hayes, who identified the people in the photo. This photo was taken about 1910-1915.
