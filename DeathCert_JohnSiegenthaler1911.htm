From the Death Certificate for John Siegenthaler

Akron, Summit County, Ohio #5700

 

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

Male, white

birth:  October 16, 1871*

age:  39 years, 3 months, 15 days

Single

birthplace:  Ohio

occupation:  rubber worker

father:  Unknown, born in Switzerland

mother:  Unknown, born in Switzerland

informant:  Jesse Hossman, Malaga, Ohio

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  January 31, 1911

physician's statement:  I HEREBY CERTIFY, That I attended deceased from Jan. 26, 1931 to Jan. 31, 1931, that I saw him alive on Jan. 30, 1931, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 4 a.m.

cause of death:  ordema (acute   of glottis)**

duration:  8 hours

contributory:  bronchitis – laryngitis  

duration:  5 days

signed:  Sam’l E. McMaster, MD, Jan. 31, 1911, Akron, Ohio

burial:  Hannibal Ohio, February 1, 1911

undertaker:  The Billow Sons Co., Akron Ohio    Chas. F. Billow

 

Submitter's Notes:   

     *   birthdate is incorrect according to 7 Jul 1870 census – he is listed as age 2

     ** handwriting is hard to read
