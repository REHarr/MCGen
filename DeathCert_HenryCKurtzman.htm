From the Death Certificate for Henry C. Kurtzman

#51231 Salem Township, Monroe County, Ohio

12 years in city where death occurred

no service in U.S. Navy or Army

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married

birth:  July 31, 1880, Salem Township, Ohio

age:  52 years 2 days

farmer

father:  Irvin Kurtzman, born Salem Township, Ohio

mother Emeline Harrison, born Ohio Township, Ohio

informant:  H. E. Kurtzman, Clarington, Ohio

burial:  Clarington Cem.; Aug 5, 1932

undertaker: W. P. Fietz*, Clarington, Ohio

filed; 8/ 5/ 1932; J.P. Ollom, Registrar

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Aug. 3, 1932

I hereby certifty, That I attended deceased  August 3, 1932 to about 3 p.m., 1932, I never saw him alive on August 3, 1932; death is said to have occurred on the date stated above at 5:30 a.m.

cause:  a self inflicted gunshot wound through center of forehead by 25 caliber stevens rifle

autopsy:  no

signed:  C. J. Fiest, coroner, Monroe County; Aug. 3, 1932; Clarington, Ohio

 

Submitter’s Note: This writing was difficult to read and may be in error.
