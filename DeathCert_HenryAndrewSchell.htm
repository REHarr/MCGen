From the Death Certificate for Henry Andrew Schell:

 

Henry Andrew Schell

Death  certificate #4929 was issued in Summit Township of Monroe County, Ohio.  Henry Andrew Schell was born June 1, 1853, in Monroe County, Ohio, lived 80 years, 7 months & 18 days.  He was a farmer and born in Monroe County, Ohio.  He was widowed.  His wife was Rosana Schell. His parents were Andrew, born in Pennsylvania, and Mary Elizabeth Roth, born in Germany.  The informant was Josephine A. Schell from Lewisville, Ohio.  [He had a daughter Josephine Augusta Schell.] Dr. O. C. Jackson of Woodsfield ascertained that the cause of death was general arteriosclerosis and senility.  He was buried in the Middle Church Cemetery on Jan. 21, 1934.

 

 [Submitters Note: He is listed as Heinrich Henry Adam Schell on the tree.]
