Passenger Ship List

 

Georg Burkhart (1797-1868), wife Magdalena Paul (1807-1850) and their six children left Germany via Harve, France aboard the ship Scotland.  They arrived in Baltimore, MD July 29, 1840 and settled in Monroe County.
