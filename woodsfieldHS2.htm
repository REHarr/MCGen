WOODSFIELD HIGH SCHOOL

 

 

This photo of the Woodsfield High School building in Woodsfield, Ohio was taken in the 1950s when it served as the main high school for Woodsfield and surrounding areas.  It no longer serves as a high school.  Woodsfield students now go to the Monroe County Central High School.  Click here for the latitude and longitude coordinates of this school.
