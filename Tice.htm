Bottom row:  Manuel Huffman, Melvin Hinderlong, Herman Rush, Lester Barnhouse, Catharine Martin, Bill Campbell, Mabel Martin, ? Kloetzy, Hazel Dennis, Mary Campbell (Mary Campbell is actually standing immediately behind the large bow in Hazel Dennis hair).

 

Second row:  Raymond Hinderlong, Margaret Lewis, Ruth Goddard, Pearl Huffman, Cecil Martin, Henry Dennis, David Kloetzy, Alice Goddard, Anna Hayes, Bill Hinderlong.

 

Third row:  George Schoonover, ? Campbell (below George), Kate Hayes, Bessie Huffman, Harry Hayes, Pearl Dennis, Della Kloetzy, ? Jimmie Fankhauser, John Schoonover, Dewey Huffman.

 

Note:  There are two girls between the second and third rows who have not been identified.  One is the girl immediately behind Margaret Lewis.  The other is the girl beside her who is standing between Margaret Lewis and Ruth Goddard and in the row (but it is only a 2-person row) behind Margaret and Ruth. If anyone can identify these two girls or complete the names above for which a ? has been added to indicate a first or last name is not available, please contact Karen Romick or the webmaster at Dick Harrington.
