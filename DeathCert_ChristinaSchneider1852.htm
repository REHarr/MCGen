From the Death Certificate for Christina Schneider nee Christman

#41607 Center Twp., Monroe Co., OH

 

Personal:  female, white, married, wife of Fred Schneider

Birth:  Jan 11, 1852, Lewisville O

Age:  81years 6 months 8 days

Occupation:  household duties

Father:  Lewis Christman, Germany

Mother:  Mary Schell, Lewisville O

Informant:  Edward Schneider, Woodsfield, Ohio

Burial:  Oak Lawn, Woodsfield O., July 21, 1933

Undertaker:  L. J. Bauer, Woodsfield O

Death:  19 July 1933

"I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from May 1, 1933 to July 9, 1933,  I last saw her alive on July 9th, 1933, death is said to have occurred on the date stated above at 10 a.m."

Cause:  fracture of hip with shock (fracture occurred June 21, 1933)

Contributory:  indigestion

Signed:  L. P. Diehl, M.D., July 19, 1933, Woodsfield O
