Unknown Subjects  Possibly a School or Church

 

Here is what appears to be a school  picture.   One guess is that it may have been taken at Antioch High School or at a church building.   On the photo above the man with the tie and vest has been written the name John L.   Could this be John Lallathin?  Can anyone recognize the site or any of the subjects?  It also looks like a bicycle wheel at the bottom left.  If anyone can recognize the site or any of the people, please contact Dorothy Bayes or Dick Harrington.
