From the Death Certificate of Jefferson Davis Staley

West Virginia State Department of Health # 13566

place of death: Wetzel County, Green District, Portersfalls, W. Va.

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL

residence: Portersfalls, Wetzel County, W. Va.

veteran: no

personal: male, white, married to Jennie Staley age 74

birth: May 13, 1863, Doolin, Wetzel Co.

age at death: 79 years 5 months 7 days

occupation: farmer; own farm

father: Samuel Staley born "don't know"

mother: Elizabeth Romeberg born Germany

informant: Jennie Staley,* Portersfalls, W.Va.

burial: 10-23-42, Middle Fork, Portersfalls, W.Va.

funeral director: W.E. Wharton, New Martinsville, W. Va.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATION

date of death: Oct. 20, 1942, 11:30 p.m.

statement of physician: I certify that death occurred on the date stated above, that I attended deceased from 1941 to Oct. 20, 1942, and that I last saw him alive on Sept. 1, 1942

cause of death: congestive heart failure; duration, sudden

due to: bursting valvular lesion; duration, 20 years

signed: F.E. Martin, M.D., New Martinsville, W. Va., 10-26-42

 

*Submitter's Note: JENNIE is the nickname of his wife, Virginia Elizabeth Staley.
