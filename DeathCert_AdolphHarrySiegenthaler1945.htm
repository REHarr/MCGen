From the Death Certificate for Adolph Harry Siegenthaler

Ohio Death Certificate #17006

 

PLACE OF DEATH:  Jefferson County, between Unionport & home, on county road

In this community:  2 years

USUAL RESIDENCE -- Ohio, Jefferson, Bloomindale, RFD #1

 

NAME OF DECEASED – Adolph Harry Siegenthaler

Male, white, married to Louisa Siegenthaler - age 64

Birth:  Nov. 18, 1881 *

Age:  67 years, 2 mos, 0 days         

Birthplace:  Monroe Co, Ohio

Occupation:  farmer, general farming

Father:  unknown, born in Switzerland

Mother:  Magdalena, born in Switzerland

Informant:  L A Siegenthaler, Unionport, Ohio

Burial:  1 – 29 – 45

Cemetery:  Unionport

Embalmer:  Dean E. Tiller

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATION

Death:  January 18, 1945, 12 noon

Cause of death:  coronary occulsion 6-23-45 due to chronic arterial sclerosis 8 years ago

Physician's statement:  I HEREBY CERTIFY, That I attended deceased from coroner case.

Signed:  Dr. John P. Smarrella, coroner, Steubenville, Ohio; 1/19/1945

 

Submitter’s Notes:

* date is wrong.  He was in the June, 1880 census as 8 years old.  Not sure if its Nov. 18, 1871 or Dec 18, 1881
