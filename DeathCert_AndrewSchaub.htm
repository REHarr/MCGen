From the Death Certificate for Andrew Schaub

Ohio Department of Health #4706

place of death: Center Twp., Monroe Co., OH

residence: near Woodsfield, (rural), Monroe Co., Ohio

PERSONAL

male, white, widowed

birth: April 11, 1861,* Lewisville, Ohio

age at death: 86 years 9 months 0 days

father: name and birthplace (don't know)

mother: name and birthplace (don't know)

informant: Thelma M. Matz, Woodsfield, Ohio

burial: Friendship Cemetery, Lewisville, Ohio

embalmer: L.J. Bauer

funeral director: Bauer & Turner, Woodsfield, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATION

date of death: Jan. 11, 1947; 6 a.m.

statement of physician: I hereby certify that I attended the deceased from Jan. 5, 1945 to Jan 11, 1947; that I last saw him alive on Dec. 31, 1946; and that death occurred on the date and hour stated above.

immediate cause of death: softning** of brain

due to: general arteriosclerosis & myocarditis

due to: advanced age

signature: O.C Jackson, M.D.; Woodsfield, Ohio; Jan. 11, 1946***

 

Submitter's Notes

* 86 years 9 months computes to 1860; 1860 is also on the tombstone, church , and other records.

** This was difficult to read and may not be correct.

*** The physician wrote 1946 although it was 1947.
