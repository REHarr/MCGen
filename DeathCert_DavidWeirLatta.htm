From the Death Certificate for David W. Latta:

 

Death certificate, #49898, was for David W. Latta of Wayne Township, who was born in Monroe Co., Ohio on Aug. 5, 1867. He lived 63 years and 4 days and was a farmer. His parents were Samuel Latta of Pa. and Hanna Okey of Ohio. The informant was his wife, Rachel Latta, of Woodsfield, Ohio. He died Aug. 9, 1930, of endocarditic myocarditis. Heart failure was a contributory cause. The signing doctor was R. H. Latta [a cousin] from Graysville who attested that he had been attending the deceased from July 20 until his death Aug. 9 at 12:30 p.m. David was buried in Woodsfield on Aug 11, 1930.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
