There are two theories regarding the father of the four Stewart men identified as brothers in Greene County, Pennsylvania.  Harold Leckey in The TenMile Country claims their father was Daniel Stewart, an Indian trader in western Pennsylvania.  A second theory claims the father is John Stewart born 1740 in Scotland.  This is based on a statement in a history of Monongalia County, West Virginia, which identifies the father of Isaac Stewart as the above John.
