From the Death Certificate of James Wilson Morris

West Virginiat State Department of Health #42

PLACE OF DEATH:  Arcola, Glade District, Webster Co., W. Va.

PERSONAL

male, white, single

birth: Feb. 14, 1919; Arcola, Webster Co., W. Va.

age at death:  1 month 8 days

occupation:  none

father:  John J. Morris, born Monroe Co., Ohio

mother:  Sarah Green Morris, born Webster Co., W. Va.

informant:  John J. Morris, Arcola, W. Va.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  March 22, 1919

statement of physician:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased on March 21, 1919, that I last saw him  alive on March 21, 1919, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 5 a.m.

cause of death:  broncho-pneumonia; duration, 1day

signature:  J.B. Dodrill, M.D.; March 24, 1919; Cowen, W. Va.

burial:  Arcola, W. Va., March 23, 1919
