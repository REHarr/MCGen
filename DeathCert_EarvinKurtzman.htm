From the death certificate for Earvin Kurtzman

#77104, Salem Twp.,  Monroe Co., OH

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

Did not serve in U.S. Navy or Army.

male, white, widower,

birth:  May 3, 1847, Monroe Co., Ohio

age:  81 years, 6 months, 28 days

farmer

father:  Fred Kurtzman, born Germany

mother:  Barbara Shell, born Pa.

informant:  H.E. Kurtzman, Clarington, O.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Dec. 1, 1928

I hereby certify that I attended deceased from Oct 10, 1928, to Dec. 1, 1928, that I last saw him alive on Oct 10, 1928, and that death occurred, on the date stated above, at 9 a.m.

cause of death:  productive* nephritis; duration 4 yrs.

contributory:  senility; duration 10 yrs. 1 mos.

operation:  no

autopsy:  no

signed:  C.A. Smith, M.D., Dec 3, 1928; Clarington, Ohio

burial:  Clarington Cemetery, Dec. 3, 1928

undertaker:  Friest* & Spring, Clarington, Ohio

* Submitter's Note:  I am not positive on the spellings of these words.
