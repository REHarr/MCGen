Owl Hollow School in 1930

 

Owl Hollow School in Spring of 1930

 

This photo carried the date of April 30, 1930.  The school went out of service about 1932 and was subsequently torn down.

 

One of the teachers who taught at the Owl Hollow School was Ora Crum Weber
