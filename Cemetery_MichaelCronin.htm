Michael Cronin Cemetery

 

The Michael Cronin Cemetery is one of the casualties of time in Monroe County. There are no longer any tombstones on the site. This cemetery is believed to be the final resting place of Michael Cronin, one of the early pioneers in Monroe County. An effort will be made to locate the old cemetery and measure the latitude and longitude before it fades completely from memory.
