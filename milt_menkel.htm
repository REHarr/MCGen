MILTONSBURG Menkel Shop

 

 

 

MILTONSBURG Menkel Shop. Photo taken in early 1900s.

 

This photo of the Charles Menkel Furniture Shop and Undertaking business was preserved on silk. These photos although faded have survived about 100 years. The process by which they were made is no longer known.
