Old Cameron or Hartline Cemetery

 

The Old Cameron Cemetery is also called the Hartline Cemetery.  It is located in Adams Township southwest of Cameron about 700 feet northwest of the bridge over Sunfish Creek.  A steep trail goes north up the hill for about 150 feet.  The cemetery is no longer maintained.  There are about 50 graves in the cemetery with many of the monuments being those of the Hartline family.
