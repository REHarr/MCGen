BARNHART  SCHOOL SITE

 

 

 

Barnhart School - 2002

 

The Barnhart School site is located about three and one-half miles north of Lewisville on the old Miltonsburg Road (presently known as the Lewisville-Northern Road or County Road 18) in the Middle Church community. The school no longer exists.  It was described as a frame, one-room structure with a slate roof .  It is not known when the building was removed but it was gone by 1950. The 4-H Club converted the site into a miniature park.  This photo was taken December 28, 2002.
