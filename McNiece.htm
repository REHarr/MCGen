1.  JAMES1 MCNIECE was born 1813 in County Londonderry, Ireland, and died 21 April 1873 in Graysville, Monroe County,  Ohio.  He married MARTHA MATILDA CRAWFORD Abt. 1834 in Ireland.  She was born 1808 in Ireland, and died 13 September 1878 in Graysville, Monroe County,  Ohio.

 

Notes for JAMES MCNIECE:

The McNiece name is originally found in the southern County of (London)Derry near the River Bann in an area known as Ballyniece. The bally part of the name, which is Irish for town or gathering,  indicates where the family's historic location can be found.
