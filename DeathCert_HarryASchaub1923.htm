From the Death Certificate for Harry A. Schaub,

#73243, Summit Twp., Monroe Co., OH

Born Feb. 9th, 1884, in Kuhn, Ohio

Age: 39 years 10 months 9 days

Single Farmer

Son of Henry Schaub and Mary Haga, both born in Kuhn, Ohio

Informant: Lillian A. Schaub,* of Kuhn, Ohio

Date of death: 12/17/1923

Cause of death: Unknown

Signing physician: W. P. Johnson of Woodsfield, Ohio

Burial: Friendship Cemetery, Lewisville, Ohio, Dec. 20, 1923

 

*Note: Harry had a sister, Lillian Antoinne (Toni) Schaub who married Charles Stewart.
