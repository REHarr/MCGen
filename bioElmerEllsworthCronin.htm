Elmer Ellsworth Cronin

 

 

 

Elmer Ellsworth Cronin did many things during his life.  Primarily, he was a grade school teacher.   He also drilled for oil in and around the Woodsfield area. He was a purchasing agent for farm materials.  He also delivered the " Monroe County Beacon Newspaper" as well as the "Ohio Weekly Newspaper" throughout the years.
