Welcome to the home page of the Monroe County, Ohio History and Genealogy. For your convenience, there are three recommended approaches for navigating this site. How to Search this Website

 

There are two options to SEARCH THIS WEB SITE

 

The first option is to use the search engine below. Simply type in your desired search term and press Google Search.

 

The second option is to search individual pages or files by following the steps found by clicking on Search. Then, you can return to this page or go to other pages on this web site by clicking on the desired page from the index that follows the instruction for searching.
