Biographical Sketch of Karl Edward Dougherty

 

Karl Edward Ed Dougherty

Source:  THE MONROE COUNTY DOUGHERTY CLAN, by James McKitrick, 1989, p 174

          Karl Edward, called Ed, was born on 7 February 1928 in Parkersburg, West Virginia.  Ed attended Jefferson Elementary School and graduated from Parkersburg High School.  Ed chose to serve his country by joining the US Marine Corps.  His basic training was at Parris Island and he was later attached to the 3rd Marines Air  Wing, MAG-33 VMP-254.  His wing was a photographic Squadron serving in the Solomon Islands during WWII.  Ed attended Mountain State College, Parkersburg, taking

Business Administration courses, and David Lipscomb College, Nashville, Tennessee, taking Economics and Social Studies.  He married Hazel June Wynn in Nashville, Tennessee on 7 February 1953.  Hazel was born on 21 June 1934 in Nashville, Tennessee.  Ed is in Banking and Financing Management with Ford and later in credit usions.  Ed and Hazel were divorced in May 1960, leaving the children with Ed to raise.  Ed is deeply involved with the Dougherty Association.  He has served as both President and Vice President of the Association, helped with the reunions from the beginning, and encouraged the upkeep of the Dougherty Cemetery.  Ed is a member of the Church of Christ and currently lives in Parkersburg, West Virginia.
