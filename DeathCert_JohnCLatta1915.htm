From the Death Certificate for John C. Latta

#62134 Malaga Twp., Monroe Co., OH*

 

Married

Born Aug. 13, 1837, W Va

Age:  78 years 2 months 27 days

Farmer

Son of John Latta and Anna Hillbury, both from Pa.

Informant:  T. L. Latta,** Calais, Ohio

Date of death:  November 10, 1915

Cause of death:  No physician called.  Third Stroke Paralysis

Burial:  Calais, Nov 12th. 1915

 

Notes:

* This certificate was marked RETURNED.  The 2d one was in completely different, large, flowing handwriting and even the signature of the informant

was not the same.  The new one had this additional statement under the "Third Stroke Paralysis:  "As to the information . . .? . . . he died a natural death."  [Submitter  had difficulty reading the one or two words.]

** T. L. Latta was his son Thomas Leroy Latta.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
