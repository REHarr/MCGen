From the Death Certificate for Infant Stalder*

Monroe Co., Ohio # 33779

unnamed Infant Stalder*

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white

birth: June 6, 1910; Sardis

age: 0 years 0 months 0 days

father: Wm. F. Stalder, born Ohio

mother: Lena Hed_______**

informant: F. Stalder; Sardis, O.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: June 6, 1910

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from June 6, 1910 to June 6 1910; that I last saw him still born 1910; and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 4 a.m.

cause of death: Still Birth

certifying physician: L. W. McKelvey, M.D.; June 7, 1910; Hannibal, O.

burial: Sardis Cemetery; June 6, 1910

 

Submitter's Notes:

* On the tombstone in the Sardis Cemetery his name was Lewis.

** The rest of the name was too faint to read, but it should have been HEDINGER.
