Thomas Leroy Latta (b. 18 Jul 1866; d. 3 May 1925) and Mary Adelia (Burris) Latta (b. 1873;m.1898; d. 1950)

 

Tom Latta taught at several Monroe County schools before becoming the county school examiner in 1910.

 

Mary taught at Calais School 1899-1901 and was at Fox Hollow in 1911.  She and her husband Tom had one child, Thomas Burris Latta, who was born and died in 1896.

 

 

(Photo taken about 1894)
