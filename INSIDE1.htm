INSIDE VIEW # 1 OF THE FORMER ST. PETER'S GERMAN EVANGELICAL PROTESTANT CHURCH AT LEWISVILLE, OHIO AS A TIRE SHOP

 

 

This is an inside view of the former St. Peter's German Evangelical Protestant Church of Lewisville, Ohio taken in 2002 after it was converted into an automotive garage and tire shop.  The church ceased to function because the aging congregation was unable to support it.  St. Peters of Lewisville joined two other churches in similar circumstances and in September of 1962, consolidated to form the Trinity United Church of Christ.  This building is located at latitude N 39o 45.801' and longitude W 081o 13.116'.
