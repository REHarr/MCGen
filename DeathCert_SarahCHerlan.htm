From the Death Certificate for Sarah C. Herlan

 

Sarah C. Herlan

Death certificate #57353 was issued in Newport Township of Washington County, Ohio.   Sarah C. Herlan, widowed from Jacob Herlan, was born Mar. 7, 1850.  She lived 73 years 6 months and 8 days.  She did housework.  She was born in Washington Co., Ohio, the daughter of  John McCoy from Pa. and Mary Scott from Pa.  Mrs. R.G. Hughes of Eureka, W Va. was the informant.  [Sarah had a daughter Sadie who was married to Robert Hughes.] Dr. E. W. LeFever signed that she died Sept 15, 1923, from intersusception of the bowels from which she had been suffering for seven days.  She was buried in Beech Grove cemetery  in Marietta, Ohio,  on Sept. 18th, 1920.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
