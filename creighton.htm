CREIGHTON RIDGE CHURCH OF CHRIST

 

 

The Creighton Ridge Church of Christ is located about a mile outside of Sycamore Valley on Route # 260.  There is a large cemetery behind the church and an even larger cemetery across Route # 260.  The church is located at latitude N 39 39.883 and longitude W 081 15.614 at elevation 1116 feet above sea level.
