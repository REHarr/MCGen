From the Death Certificate for George L. Young

State of Ohio #21087

place of death: Woodsfield, Monroe County, Ohio

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, widowed

birth: April 10, 1845, Pittsburg [sic], Pa.

age at death: 77 years 11 months 15 days

father: name (don't know); born Germany

mother: name (don't know); birthplace (don't know)

informant: J.H. Christy,* Woodsfield, O.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: March 25, 1923

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from 3/24, 1923 to 3/25, 1923, that I last saw him alive on 3/24/1923, and that death occurred, on the date stated above, at 11:30 a.m.

cause of death: apoplexy

contributory: arteriosclerosis

where was disease contracted: at home

operation: no

autopsy: no

test confirming diagnosis: clinical

signed: D.W. Lowe, M.D.; 3/26/1923; Woodsfield, O.

burial: Oaklawn Cem.; Mar 26, 1923

undertaker: W.P. Galbraith, Woodsfield, Ohio

 

*Submitter's Note: J.H. Christy was George's stepson.
