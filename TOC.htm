Monroe County History & Genealogy (MCHG) Web Site

 

There are a number of ways to navigate the Monroe County History and Genealogy (MCHG) web site.  One way is to go to the home page that has a series of buttons that will take you to various sections and pages of the site.  Another is to use the list/index of the most frequently used sections that is included at the bottom of most pages on the site.  Below is still another option.  This Table of Contents (TOC) is designed to provide at a glance all of the sections and pages on this site.  It has the advantage of showing how the various sections and pages are related.  By clicking on any blue highlighted item in the TOC, you will be transported to that item.  By clicking on the term Table of Contents in the Short-cut Table of Contents found at the bottom of each web-page, you can return to this TOC.
