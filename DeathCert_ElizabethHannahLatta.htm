From the Death Certificate for Elizabeth Hannah Latta

#39532, Wayne Twp., Monroe Co.,OH

 

Birth:  Nov. 25, 1838, Ohio

Age:  72 years 8 months 1 day

Married

Housework

Daughter of James Okey and Catherine Agin, both born Ohio

Informant:  D. W. Latta,* Woodsfield, O

Date of death:  July 26, 1911

Cause of death:**

Certifying physician:  A.F. Latta,*** Graysville, Ohio

Burial:  Moose Ridge, Ohio, July 27, 1911

 

Notes:

*The informant was her son David Weir Latta.

** The cause of death was unreadable on this copy of the death certificate.

*** Allen Forerst Latta was her nephew, the son of John C. Latta and Mary Jane Morris.
