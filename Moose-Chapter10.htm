5. Lela Marie Moose17,18 ( John Sr., George, John J, Clifford), (Thomas Hall, John M.)

 

Lela was the oldest child and was born in Monroe County on17 Aug 189618. On Nov 12, 1915 she married Ernest R. Bates in Monroe County16. Probate Judge E. J. Thompson performed the ceremony. Ernest Bates was born on 5 July 1890 in Wayne Township18 and was the son of Richard Atherton Bates and Rebecca Letetia Dougherty66. Some time after 1917 and before 1920 the family moved to Oklahoma where Ernest drilled oil wells on one of the Indian Reservations45, 67.

 

 

Ernest and Lela returned to Monroe County after 1920 and Ernest became a farmer. They had three children all born in Monroe County.

 

Probably before 1940, Ernest and Lela moved to Ashland County, Ohio and purchased a farm near Polk on County Rd.601. They later purchased a second farm in Orange Township which was bordered on the north by Township Rd. 700 and on the west by State Rd. 58 and farmed them both for several years45.

 

Ernest and Lela retired on their second farm along Ohio Route 58. Lela died on 22 Dec 1987 at the age of 91 years 4 months and 5 days. Ernest Bates died on 21 Aug 1989 at the age of 99 years 1 month and 16 days37,45, 70.
