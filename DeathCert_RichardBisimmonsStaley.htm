From the Death Certificate for Richard Bisimmons Staley

West Virginia Death Certificate #539

Hancock County, Clay District, New Cumberland, W.Va.

Richard B. Staley

residence: New Cumberland, W.Va.; 38 years

 

Personal and Statistical Particulars

male, white, married to Alice Davis

birth: Nov. 26,  1880, Wetzel Co., WVa

age at death: 48 years, 1 month, 14 days

occupation: brick layer, Crescent Brick Company

father:  Martin V. Staley, born Wheeling, WVa

mother: Mary A. Thomas,  born Wetzel Co., WVa

informant: Paul Staley, New Cumberland, WVa

 

Medical Certificate of Death

date of death:  Jan. 9, 1929

physician’s statement: I hereby certify that I attended deceased from Jan. 5, 1929 to Jan 9, 1929, that I last saw him alive on Jan 9, 1929, and that death occurred on date state above at 9 a.m.

cause of death: influenza

duration: 4 da.

no operation

no autopsy

signing physician: Arthur E. McClue, M.D., New Cumberland, WVa

burial: Jan 11, 1929, New Cumberland, WVa

undertaker: Fred T. Badley,*  New Cumberland, WVa

 

*Submitter’s Note: This name was difficult to read and may not be the correct spelling.
