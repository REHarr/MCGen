BINDING PROJECT FOR RESEARCH ROOM

If you have been visiting the Research Room in the Monroe County District Library over the years, you will have noticed some changes. When the collection was first placed in the Research Room at the library around 1987, most of the articles were soft covered books or donated information that was placed in folders. After our chapter began the sole maintenance of the room in 1988, we embarked on a project to hard bind all of the items in the room. This preserved the items by increasing the durability of the items and it also added to the attractiveness of the room. As the size of the collection continued to grow, the number of items that needed binding increased. Over the past few years, we have been fortunate to be the recipient of many generous donations made by our members. Each year when they renew their membership, many of them also contribute financially to this project. You can also be a part of the project and help us preserve Monroe CountyÂ’s genealogical heritage.

We welcome donations in any amount. We have received donations ranging from $3.00 to $200.00. Every donation is appreciated and has helped us on toward our goal. Each item costs about $8.00 to hard bind. If you would like to donate, please send it to the Hard Binding Project, c/o Monroe Chapter OGS, Box 641, Woodsfield, OH 43793. For each book you contribute to binding, we will place your name inside the book as the donor for the binding. If you would like to donate in memory of someone, that can also be added. Over the years, our Research Room has been transformed because of generous people who wanted to help preserve our heritage.
