Biographical Sketch of James Edgar Dixon

 

James Edgar Dixon

Source: OUR BOOK: OUR ANCESTORS, OURSELVES, AND OUR CHILDREN by Ben F. Dixon, 1932: page 85.

 

     Born at Kahoka, Mo., July 31, 1899.

     Graduated, Kahoka High School, 1918.

     Graduated, Washington University School of Dentistry, St. Louis, Mo., D.D.S. degree, 1924.  Member:  National Dental Fraternity (Xi Psi Phi), B.P.O.E.; A.F. & A.M.; O.E.S.

     Missouri and Illinois State Boards of Dentistry, 1924.

     Practiced Dentistry, Hannibal, Mo., 1924 to the present.  [Submitter's Note:  present was 1932.]

     Married Alda Berghofer at the home of J. Fred Doyle, Fairfield, Iowa, Dec. 26, 1929; Rev. Voorhees, Presbyterian Minister, officiating.

     Office address:  506 Broadway, Hannibal, Mo.
