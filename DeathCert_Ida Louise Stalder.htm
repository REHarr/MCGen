From the Death Certificate for Ida Louise Stalder (nee Gauding)

Sardis, Lee Township, MonroeCo., Ohio, #44377*

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

female, w. caucasian, married to Charles O. Stalder

birth:  Apr. 15-1884; Sardis, Rt 2, Ohio

age:  55 years 3 months 1 day

trade:  housewife

father:  Charles Gauding, born Sardis Rt 2, Ohio

mother:  Minnie Hartlieb, born Sardis, Ohio

informant:  Oma Stalder; Sardis, Rt. 1, Ohio

burial:  Salem Cem.; July 19, 1939

funeral firm:  J.W. Rush & Son; Sardis, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  July 16, 1939

physician's statement:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from 4/5/38 to 7/16, 1939, I last saw her alive on May 8, 1939, death is said to have occurred on the date stated above at 7:00 a.m.

principal cause of death:  chronic myocarditis

                                         auricular fibrillation

contributory causes:  toxic thyroid

operation:  none

autopsy:  none

signed:  W. F. Mills,** M.D.; 7-16-1939; New Matamoras, Ohio

 

Submitter's Notes:

       ** This writing was difficult to read and may be in error.

 

RETURNED copy of Death Certificate #44377 for ida Louise Stalder.

"Kindly have physician state whether deceased had an exophthalmic goiter"

 

Mrs. Ida Stalder had an enlarged thyroid which had been toxic several yars ago but was not toxic within the last five years before her death.  Signed-------W. F. Mills,** M.D.; New Matamoras, O.
