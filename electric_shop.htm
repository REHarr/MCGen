Paul Riemenschneider Electric Shop moved:

Published in The Spirit of Democracy dated, Thursday, November 27, 1924

“Paul Riemenschneider, Woodsfield’s popular electrician has moved his electric shop from the McAndrews building on East Court street to the rooms recently vacated by Roy Landefeld on North Main Street, near the Farmers Milling Company.”
