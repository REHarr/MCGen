New Bake Shop

 

Published in The Spirit of Democracy
dated Thursday, September 25, 1924

Harold Stoehr, formerly with the Driggs Bakery and the New System Bakery, plans to open a bakery in the Masters building near the depot in the near future. Mr. Stoehr is a good baker, and will no doubt succeed well. With the opening of the Driggs Bakery and the New System, the people of Woodsfield can buy bakers bread in great quantities.
