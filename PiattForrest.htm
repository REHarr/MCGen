MELVIN FORREST PIATT

 

 

Melvin Forrest Piatt went by the given name of Forrest.  He was the son of John P. Piatt and Elizabeth Shriver.  Forrest was born 23 Aug 1870 and died 29 May 1941. He is buried at Mt. Zion Cemetery, Richland County, OH

 

Forrest was married on 24 September 1902 to Mary Orilla Bischoff who was the daughter of Johann Rudolph Bischoff and Catherine Luikart.  Mary was born 1 Sep 1879 and died 17 Mar 1968.  She was buried at Mt Zion Cemetery, Richland County, OH


Seven children were born to Forrest and Mary Piatt.  They were: 

 

Edna Grace Piatt born 7 Mar 1903 in Terre Haute IN

Charles Berl Piatt born 2 Jun 1905 at Beallsville, Monroe County, OH

Beatrix Lucille Piatt born 21 Dec 1908 at Beallsville, Monroe County, OH

Glenn A. Piatt born Mt. Vernon, Knox County, OH.
