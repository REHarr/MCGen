From the Death Certificate for Edward LeRoy Newhart

 

Ohio Death Certificate #58453

place of death:  306 N. Cory, Findlay, Hancock Co., Ohio

residence:  306 N. Cory St.

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married to Mary E. Newhart

birth:  July 4, 1876;* Bellaire, Ohio

age at death:  62 years 2 months 27 days

trade:  ground man for electric co.

father:  George Newhart, born Ohio

mother:  Mary Jane Forsher, born Bellaire, Ohio

informant:  Mrs. D. Abell; Findlay, Ohio

burial:  Maple Grove; Oct 3, 1938

funeral firm:  W. G. Coldren Funeral Home

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Oct. 1, 1938

physician's statement:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from June 23,         1937, to Oct. 1, 1938, I last saw him alive on Sept. 30, 1938, death is said to         have occurred on the date stated above at 3. a.m.

cause of death:  carcinoma of colon; date onset:  June, 1937

contributory cause:  bowel obstruction with toxaemia;  date onset:  July 1937

operation:  colostomy, July, 1937

test confirming diagnosis:  section

autopsy:  no

disease or injury related to occupation:  no

signature: T A. Spitler,** M.D., 10/3/1938; Findlay, O

 

submitter's notes:

        * The July 4, 1876 appears to be an error based on all other known records.

        ** Physician's signature was difficult to read and may be in error.
