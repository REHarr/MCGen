News from the Chapter

 

May meeting:

The May meeting will be held at the St. Paul’s United Church of Christ on South Main Street in Woodsfield. The meeting will begin at 2:00 p.m.

Upcoming publications:

A new cemetery publication has been planned for this summer. The cemetery inscriptions of Benton and Washington Townships will be published as our latest addition cemetery inscriptions. (See our Publications for Sale for a list of other cemetery inscription books.)

1900 Census Project

Members are working on the 1900 census for a future publication. But help is still needed! If you would like to volunteer to copy a township in the 1900 census, please contact us for a list of townships still available. We’ll look forward to hearing from you.
