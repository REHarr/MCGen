REQUEST FOR PIONEER FAMILIES

OF MONROE COUNTY MEMBERSHIP FORM

 

Are you a descendant of an individual that settled in Monroe County, Ohio by December 31, 1850? If so, you are eligible to join Pioneer Families of Monroe County. Pioneer Families does not replace First Families but works in conjunction with that group.

To join Pioneer Families of Monroe County, fill out an application and prove that you descend from an individual who settled in Monroe County by December 31, 1850. Send the application with accompanying documentation and a one time

$15.00 membership fee.

 

_______ Yes! My family was in Monroe County by December 31, 1850. Please send me an application for membership in Pioneer Families of Monroe County, Ohio.

If your family is already a member of First Families and you wish to enroll them in Pioneer Families also, please send an application noting your First Families status and a one time $10.00 membership fee.
