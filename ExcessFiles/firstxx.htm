REQUEST FOR FIRST FAMILIES OF MONROE COUNTY MEMBERSHIP FORM

 

Are you a descendant of an individual that settled in Monroe County, Ohio by December 31, 1830? If so, you are eligible to join First Families of Monroe County Ohio.

To join First Families of Monroe County, fill out an application and prove your descent from an individual who settled in Monroe County by December 31, 1830. Send the application with accompanying documentation and a one time $15.00 membership fee to the Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society at the address below.
