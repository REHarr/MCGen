From the death certificate for Conrad John Goodballet

#44040 Girard, Trumbull Co., OH

Residence: 112 Smithsonian St.

 

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, widower

birth: June 23--1864; Marietta, Ohio

age: 64 years 11 months 10 days

occupation: retired

father: Conrad John Goodballet, born Ohio

mother: not known

informant: Floyd Goodballet; Girard, Ohio

filed: 6/4, 1929; Julia Warner, Registrar

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: June 2, 1929

I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Dec. 8, 1928, to June 2, 1929, that I last saw him alive on June 1, 1929, and that death occurred, on the date stated above, at 8:30* a.m.

cause of death: Arteriosclerosis; duration: 6 yrs.

contributory: Apoplexy; duration: 5 years

autopsy: no

signed: J. E. King, M.D.; June 3, 1929; Girard, O.

burial: Liberty Union Cemetery; June 4, 1929

undertaker: Blackstone & Reese; Girard

 

Submitter's Note:

* This could possibly be 8:50 a.m.
