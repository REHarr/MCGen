The Parry Museum, which is located at 217 Eastern Avenue, was originally built as a home in approximately 1842 and owned by Nathan Hollister.  Hollister then sold the ten-room home to Dr. John R. Parry in 1889.  In 1974, the Monroe County Park District bought the house from Georgia Parry, daughter of Dr. John Parry.  The museum was first opened on July 4, 1976, showing only the two rooms it had re-finished.  The last room was completed in 1982.
