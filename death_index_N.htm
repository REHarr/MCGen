Members of MCC of OGS and visitors to this site have submitted the following death records for posting. You are invited to submit death records for posting. Death records are posted alphabetically as they are received. In addition to using the alphabetical index of surnames below, you can also use the search engine on the home page of this web site. To post a death record send it by e-mail to Richard Harrington. Please use the format in the examples below.

       Note: In the table(s) below, the date of birth normally appears as a date (month, day and year). However, in some cases, particularly for the older records, the date of death is given and the age of the person at the time of death. In these cases, this information has been recorded in the column titled, Date of Birth. The first number is the age in years. The second and third numbers are the age in months and days, respectively. In some cases, the month or day may be missing. So, the information has been recorded as it was received and it is up to the reader to decide if the number is the month or day.
