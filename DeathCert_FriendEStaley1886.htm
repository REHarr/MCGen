From the Death Certificate of Friend E. Staley

West Virginia State Department of Health # 13356

place of death:  Dist. No. 5251; Wetzel County, Green District, Porters Falls, B & O  R.R. Track

residence:  Porters Falls

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

personal:  male, white, married to Mary McCormack Staley

birth:  May 30, 1886, Wetzel Co., W.Va.

age at death:  49 years 3 months 2 days

occupation:  engineer at Gas Compressing Plant

date last worked:  Sept. 2, 1935

father:  Jefferson Staley born Wetzel Co., W.Va.

mother:  Virginia Thomas born Wetzel Co., W.Va.

informant:  Mary Staley, Porters Falls

burial:  Morgan Cem.; Sept. 4, 1935

undertaker:  W.E. Wharton, New Martinsville

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Sept. 2, 1935, 3:08 p.m.

cause:  struck by B & O Train

external cause:  accident, Sept 2, 1935

manner of injury:  run over by train

nature of injury:  body mangled

signed:  L.W.  Dulaney, J.P., Acting Coroner; 280 Virginia St., New Martinsville, W.Va.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
