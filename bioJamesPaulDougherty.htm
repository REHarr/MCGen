Biographical Sketch of James Paul Dougherty

 

James Paul Dougherty

Source:  MONROE COUNTY OHIO DOUGHERTY CLAN, by James McKitrick, 1989, p 175

            James Paul Dougherty was born on 30 August 1936 at Parkersburg, West Virginia.  He attended Jefferson Elementary School and graduated from Parkersburg High School.  James, called Jim, enlisted in the USAF and was attached to Sampson AFB, New York.  He married Barbara Ann Locke on 13 October 1959.  Barbara was from Texas.  After his military service, Jim was a fireman for the city of Parkersburg, then a mechanic for American Airlines.  Jim and Barbara live in Fort Worth, Texas.  Jim is a member of the Church of Christ.
