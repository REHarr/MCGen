The Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society maintains a well stocked Research Room in the Monroe County District Library, 96 Home Avenue, Woodsfield, 43793 -- Phone: (740) 472-1954). Our chapter maintains the collection of data on Monroe County and related areas. Donations are welcome.

Hours: Monday - Thursday, 10:00 - 8:00; Friday - Saturday 10:00 - 5:00

 The Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society maintains an impressive collection of microfilm, microfiche, and CDs for the use of our members and guests. The chapter also maintains readers for the microfilm and microfiche at the library. Click on the hyperlink immediately below to see a list of these items.

Some of the items to be found in the Research Room include the following:

Family Trees and related family history documents

First Family Records

Pioneer family Records.

Reference Books

Surname Files

Microfilm Reader

Microfilms and microfiche

CDs

[Much, much more]

Click here for directions and maps for getting to the Research Room located in the Monroe County Public Library

While the Research Room is open to researchers at all times the library is open, it is only staffed Monday through Friday from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.  At others times researchers may work independently.  If you have questions during times when the room is not staffed, you may call Karen Romick at her home at (740) 483-1481 and she will try to assist you.

The Research Room receives many visitors each year. It is operated and maintained by local volunteers. If you would like to help maintain the MCC of OGS Research Room, please contact Karen Romick at:
