Monroe County, Ohio Record Books Available on CDs

 

Click here for a printable CD Order Form

 

A special project has been initiated to make important historical records of Monroe County, Ohio available to researchers and to the general public at a reasonable price.  Entire record books have been photographed and put onto CDs.  Every detail of each page and of each record has been captured in the photos of these record books.  By working with these CDs every detail of information is at your fingertips.  In most cases, the price of each CD is far less than the cost to travel to the Monroe County Courthouse in Woodsfield, Ohio.  The high pixel photography used in these photos provides outstanding reproduction of each page.  In most cases, using the enlargement or zoom feature of your computer results in the ability of figure out handwriting easier than from the original document.  And, all this can be done in the comfort of your own home using nothing more than the CD-ROM drive of your own computer.

 

CDs of these valuable record books are available from the Monroe County Historical Society (MCHS).  Orders can be placed by sending a letter containing the order form at the end of this page and your check to:

 

Monroe County Historical Society

P.O. Box 538

Woodsfield, Ohio 43793-0538

 

Phone:  740/472-1933

 

Be sure to include your full name, full mailing address including zip code, phone number, and e-mail address (1).  Your check or money order should be made payable to the Monroe County Historical Society.  (Sorry, we cannot accept credit cards.  Please do not send cash.)  Please understand that we cannot ship your CDs until payment has been received.  CDs will normally be shipped within 2 working days and should reach you within 7 to 10 working days following your order.

 

If you prefer, you can print an order form from our Publications list on the MCHS website.  The address of the MCHS website is:  http://www.rootsweb.com/~ohmchs/   You can also e-mail MCHS at  MCHS2000@1st.net .

Note (1)    The CDs of record books are prepared from photographs of pages from the actual record books.  Some of these books have an index of their contents.  Other books are subdivided into sections according to the alphabet.  The CDs include the index where they are available and the alphabetical organization is preserved for those books that do not have an index.  In the future (over the next 2 years) we expect to construct a computer searchable index (CSI) for many of the record books.  This will greatly enhance the value of the CDs to researchers by allowing your computer to do the searching (by name) for you.  Because the present CDs do not have a CSI we plan to offer to purchasers of the presently available CDs, an upgrade (at cost  about $3.50) as the CSIs become available.  Please include your e-mail address so that we can notify you when these upgraded CDs are available.  Your e-mail address will be secure with us.  We do not sell or otherwise compromise the security of clients personal information or e-mail addresses.

 

Note (2)    Ohio residents please add $0.75 for each CD purchased to cover Ohio and county taxes.  CDs can not be shipped until payment has been received.

 

Note (3)    Price includes shipping and handling.
