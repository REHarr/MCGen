NOTES ABOUT EVAN HANEY:


1850 Monroe Co., OH Census:  His name is spelled Evin.  Also, in this census, there is no wife listed.  A 19 year old female named Acious Cline is listed in the household.  His occupation is listed as "farmer."   [in probate papers, a grandson named Evan Cline is listed as a beneficiary.]
---
Burial: Evan and Hannah are buried in Mt Hope Cemetary, Haney Ridge, Marr, Ohio. It is a little Cemetary across the road from Mt. Hope Baptist church. They are buried with the Durbin Family.
---
Property: 39.74 acres in Washington Township, Monroe County.  S.E. Quarter of S.W. Quarter of section 20 in Township 4 of range 6 in District of Marrietta.
