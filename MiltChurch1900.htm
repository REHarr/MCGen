St. Peters Evangelical Church and Parsonage - Miltonsburg - about 1900

St. Peters Evangelical Church of Miltonsburg as it appeared in about 1900.  The parsonage of the St. Peters Evangelical Church of Miltonsburg is in the background on the left.  Both structures still exist in the year 2002.  The church has been abandoned and is currently used for storage.  The parsonage is now a private residence.  The church is on Lot # 39.  The parsonage is on Lot # 40.  This photo is on silk and was likely taken about 1900.
