From the Death Certificate for Peter Herlan:

 

Peter Herlan

Death certificate #75779 was issued in Newport Township of Washington County, Ohio.  Peter Herlan was born Nov. 10th, 1837.  He lived 93 years, 1 month, 11 days.  He was a widower.  He was born in Cannonsburg, Guernsey Co., Ohio, and was a retired farmer.  He was the son of Jacob Herlan born in Germany and Jane Mendenhall born in Pa.  The informant was J. A. Herlan of Schley, Ohio.  [He had a son named John Aaron.]  Dr. Geo. H. Gale of Newport, Ohio, signed that Peter died Dec. 31, 1930, from uremia.  A contributory cause was a fracture of the neck of the femur eleven days before.  He was buried in Newport, Ohio, Jan. 3, 1931.  The certificate was marked RETURNED with this typed message:  "Kindly have physician state the manner in which the fracture was received and whether accidental."  Dr. Gale's written response was, "Triped [sic] on carpet and fell on floor --accidental."
