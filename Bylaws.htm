Article X: Amendments

 

This constitution may be amended by a two-thirds vote of the members present at any meeting of the chapter, provided notice of the proposed amendment has been given to all members, either at a previous meeting or through a prior issue of the newsletter.

 

Article XI: Parliamentary Procedure

 

Robert's Rules of Order, Revised, when not in conflict with this Constitution and By Laws and Standing Rules, shall govern the proceedings of this organization.

1.     This chapter is organized exclusively for the purpose set forth in Article II, subject to the provisions of Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Service Code.
