From the Death Certificate for John M. Neuhart

Summit Twp., Monroe Co., Ohio, #17790 or 47790 [hard to read]

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, widower

birth: Aug. 28, 1820, Europe

age: 89 years, 0 months, 13 days

parents: born Euope

informant: Luzetta Neuhart, Lewisville, Ohio**

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: Sept 11, 1909 at 4 a.m.

cause of death: old age

signed: J.W. Weber, M.D.; Sept 11, 1909, Lewisville, Ohio

burial: Monroe Field M.E. Church; Sept 13, 1909

undertaker: Chas. Menkel, Miltonsburg, Ohio

 

**Submitter s Note: Luzetta Neuhart may have been his daughter-in-law who is listed on the tree at Monroe Co., Ohio, as Lucetta (Bintz), the wife of James Jacob Neuhardt.
