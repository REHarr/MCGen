Paul Young found the following records in an old Bible. Since these names were not among the Dougherty's listed on the website he thought they may be of help to someone.


Dougherty, Rebecca L.

b. August 16, 1870

Dougherty, Edward Everett

b. February 26, 1873

Dougherty, Forrest Delbert

b. October 6, 1875

Dougherty, Willard B.

b. January 13, 1877

Dougherty, Lilie? V?

b. March 25, 1879    (Name badly faded; not sure if Lilie V.)

Dougherty, Dora Ethel

b. June 3, 1882 d. September 18, 1883

Dougherty, Leona M.

b. July 12, 1884

Dougherty, Dora

b. February 18, 1887

Dougherty, Alice Elma

b. September 12, 1889

Dougherty, Herman A.

b. November 3, 1891


These names were recorded in a New Testament published in 1858. With one exception, there were no death dates, nor location of residence. The Bible was among the books in the library of Bernard C. Dillon, whose mother was Edna Dougherty Dillon. It was not clear who owned this Bible but the following inscription suggests that it probably was Edward Dougherty.

 

Edward Dougherty

Edwina, Monroe County, Ohio

 

Provided by Paul Young
