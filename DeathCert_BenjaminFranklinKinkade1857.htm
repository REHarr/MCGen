From the Death Certificate for Benjiman [sic?] Franklin Kinkade

Kahoka, Clark Co., Missouri #5075

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, widowed

husband of Susan Kinkade

birth:  July 9, 1857 in  Illinois

age at death:  78 years 7 months 29 days

occupation:  stone cutter

father:  E. S. Kinkade, born Ohio

mother:  Sarah E. Spillman, born Ind.

informant:  Mrs. Rachel Dixon; Kahoka, Mo.

burial:  Kahoka Ce.; Feb. 10, 1936

undertaker:  [not readable]

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Feb. 8, 1936

physician's statement:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Feb. 7, 1936  to Feb. 8, 1936. I last saw him alive on Feb. 8, 1936.  Death is said to  have occurred on the date stated above at 10:15 p.m.

cause of death:  angina pectoris

contributory causes:  asthma

injury related to occupation of deceased:  no

signature:  P.S. Borton, D.O.; Kahoka, Mo.
