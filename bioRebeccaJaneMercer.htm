Biographical Sketch of Rebecca Jane Mercer

 

Source: HISTORY OF TYLER COUNTY, WEST VIRGINIA TO 1984,  page 377

 

      Rebecca Jane, a younger child of Jeremiah and Alcinda, was born on Tenmile Creek.  In 1876 she married William (Wade) Lehew (1853-1928) and the two settled in Tyler County where several of their children were born.  William carried the mail on horseback along the Star Route from Middlebourne to New Martinsville.  Late in the 1880's, William & Rebecca moved to New Martinsville where William carried the mail from the train station to the post office.  They had 11 children, but several died at birth or at an early age of diphtheria and scarlet fever. 

 

          After William died of old age in New Martinsville, Rebecca lived with the family of her daughter Catherine.  She died of cancer of the face at Porter Falls in Wetzel County.  Rebecca and William are buried in the Mercer Cemetery in Tyler County.

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
