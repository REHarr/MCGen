West Union Church and Cemetery

The West Union Church of Christ and the West Union one-room School (or Lyman School) were located about 100 yards apart.  This photo was taken near the site where the school stood.  A photo taken of a church event in 1914 shows both buildings still standing.  A second photo also believed to have been taken around 1913 shows the school in the foreground.  (Note:  The above picture appears twice on the website contributed by different sources.  Click here to see the other picture.)
