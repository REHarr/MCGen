From the death certificate for Ada Nalley Kincade, #62143

Bethel Township, Monroe County, Ohio

female, white, married

birth:  Jan. 14, 1892,* Ohio

age:  22 yrs. 10 mos. 11 da.

housewife

daughter of Thomas Nalley, born Ohio, and Mary Hodge, born Ohio

informant:  Douglass Kincade,* * Graysville. . . .

death:  Nov. 25,* * * 1914, 4 p.m.

last attended by physician:  from April 1914 to Nov. 20, 1914 when last seen alive by physician

cause of death:  pulmonary tuberculosis

burial:  Pleasant R. Cem., Nov. 26, 1914

physician:  J.S. Peter * * * *

 

Submitter's Notes:

*  MONROE COUNTY, OHIO CEMETERY INSCRIPTIONS:  BENTON AND WASHINGTON TOWNSHIPS published by the Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society lists 1893 as her birth on the tombstone.

* * Douglass Kincade was her husband.  On the death certificate for their daughter who died 11 days earlier it was spelled Douglas Kinkade.

* * * The above book lists Nov. 24 as the date of death on her tombstone.

* * * * The signature of the physician was difficult to read and this spelling may not be correct.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
