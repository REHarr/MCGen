ST. PETER'S GERMAN EVANGELICAL PROTESTANT CHURCH AT LEWISVILLE, OHIO

 

 

This photo of the St. Peter's German Evangelical Protestant Church of Lewisville, Ohio was taken in 1979.  This church ceased to function because the aging congregation was unable to support it.  St. Peters of Lewisville joined two other churches in similar circumstances and in September of 1962, consolidated to form the Trinity United Church of Christ.  Eventually, this building was sold and pressed into service as an automotive garage and tire shop.
