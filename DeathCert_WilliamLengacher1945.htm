From the Death Certificate for William Lengacher

Ohio Death Certificate #36954

 

PLACE OF DEATH -- Ottawa County, Portage Twp.

In this community: 7 days

USUAL RESIDENCE -- Ohio, Wayne, rural, Sterling RD #1

 

NAME OF DECEASED William Lengacher

Death:  June 25, 1945

Male, white, married to Rosena Lengacher - age 75

Birth:  Mch 21, 1870

Age:  75 years, 3 mos, 4 days         

Birthplace:  Sardis, Ohio

Occupation:  farmer

Father:  Christian Lengacher, born in Switzerland

Mother:  Susan Schmaker, born in Switzerland

Informant:  Alston E. Baker, Gypsum, Ohio

Burial:  June 25, 1945

Cemetery:  Rittman, Ohio

Embalmer:  William F. Wolf

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATION

Cause of death:  cerebral hemorrage 6-23-45 due to chronic arterialsclerosis 8 years ago

Physician's statement:  I HEREBY CERTIFY, That I attended deceased from June 23, 1945 to June 25, 1945, that I saw him alive on June 24, 1945, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 4 a.m.

Signed:  C. J. Guisley, MD, Port Clinton, Ohio; 6/25/1945
