From the Death Certificate for John Harvey Pratt

Belmont County Volume #4976, Certificate #13368

Place of death:  Pultney Township, Belmont County, Ohio

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married to Kath__ Jane Pratt*

birth:  June 22, 1858, Ohio

age:  67 years 8 months 13 days

occupation:  laborer, Neff Lumber Co.

father:  John Pratt, born Ohio

mother:  Sarah Hartline, born Monroe County, Ohio

informant:  George Pratt; Neffs, Oh

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Mar. 5, 1926

physician's statement:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Oct. 6, 1923, to March 5, 1926,  that I last saw him alive on March 5, 1926, and that death occurred, on the date state above at 10:25 p.m.

cause of death:

     _________________________** by injury of Oct. 6, 1923 by falling from a wagon on head

duration:  2 yrs. 5 mos.

contributory:  angina pectoris

signed:  Chas. H. Cale., M.D.;  3-10, 1926; Neffs, O.

burial:  Rose Hill Cem.; 3-8, 1926

undertaker:  Grafton & Mellott; Bellaire, O.

 

Submitter's Notes:

        *MONROE COUNTY OHIO MARRIAGES 1867-1917 listed his marriage to Catharine J. Rouse.  Her name  was illegible on the death certificate after the first 4 letters.

        **There were several words here which could not be read.
