From the Death Certificate for Acel Leach

#11868,* Seneca Twp., Monroe Co., OH

 

Widowed

Birth:  July 22, 1847, Ohio

Age:  70 years 6 months 17 days

Retired Farmer

Parents:  Benj Leach** and Susan Mahony, both born Ohio

Informant:  W. T. Newhart,*** Batesville, O

Date of death:  Feb. 9, 1918

Cause of Death:  No physician called; infirmaties [sic]

Burial:  Calais, Ohio, Feb 11, 1918

Undertaker:  G.H. Herlan, Summerfield

 

Notes:

* The original death certificate was marked RETURNED requiring:  "Signature of Registrar and date of  filing of this certificate."  The new one signed by W.W. McVay said:  "I issued permit for this on the testimony of G.H. Herlan, undertaker and forwarded the certificate to W.N. Wehr for record. Signed:  May 9th 1918."

** The name of the father listed here, BENJ, is in conflict with other records stating it was William Leach.

*** W.T. Newhart could possibly be the husband of his daughter Stella, William Franklin Neuhart.  Perhaps the initials are W.F. not W.T.  The signature is in the same handwriting as most of the blanks on this form.  Other documents show the spelling of his son-in-law as Neuhart.
