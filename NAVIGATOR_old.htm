The Navigator is the quarterly publication of the Monroe County Chapter of Ohio Genealogical Society (MCC of OGS). The Navigator has been awarded by the Ohio Genealogical Society for its excellence as a chapter newsletter. Issues are mailed in January, April, July and October to all members of the MCC of OGS. An annual index of names of ancestors and other people that appeared in the Navigator throughout the current year is mailed in December. Queries may be placed in the Navigator free of charge to all members but all queries must pertain to Monroe County ancestors.

The Navigator has been published since 1988. Copies of back issues are available for $2.00 per issue or $10.00 per membership year. Below is a list of available issues. Requests for back issues should be addressed to The Navigator, Monroe County Chapter of OGS, P.O. Box 641, Woodsfield, OH, 43793. Requests should be accompanied with your check of $2.00 per issue or $10.00 per membership year.
