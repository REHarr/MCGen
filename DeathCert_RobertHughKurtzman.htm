From the death certificate for Robert Hugh Kurtzman

#11166, Clarington Village, Monroe County, Ohio

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white single

birth: April 30, 1925, Salem Tp., Ohio

age: 1 year 9 months 12 days

father: Hugh Kurtzman, born Salem Tp., Monroe Co., Ohio

mother: Alma Knowlton, born Tyler Co., W. Va.

informant: H. E. Kurtzman of Clarington, Ohio

filed: Feb. 15, 1927, R. E. Klech,* Registrar

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: Feb. 12, 1927

statement: I hereby certify, That I attended deceased from Feb. 4, 1927, to Feb. 12, 1927, that I last saw him alive on Feb. 12, 1927, and that death occurred, on the date stated above, at 11 p.m.

cause: pneumonia; duration, 9 ds.

contributory: whooping cough; duration, 2 mos.

signed: C.* A. Smith, M.D., Feb. 14, 1927; Clarington, Ohio

burial: Clarington Cem., Feb. 15, 1927

undertaker: Fiest* & Spring, Clarington, Ohio
