From the death certificate for Anna Ricer

#5273, Woodsfield, Monroe Co., OH

 

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

female, white, married to Wm. Ricer

birth: July 3--1870, Monroe Co., Ohio

age: 64 years 6 months 5 days

occupation: house wife

father: L.B. Christman born Germany

mother: Mary P. Schell born "don't know"

informant: Raymond Ricer; Woodsfield, Ohio

burial: Jan. 10th, 1935; Woodsfield, O

undertaker: W.P. Galbraith; Woodsfield, O

filed: Jan 9, 1935; Frank L. Buskin, * Registrar

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: Jan. 8th, 1935

I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Dec. 26,* 1934, to Jan. 8, 1935, that I last saw her alive on Jan. 8, 1935, death is said to have occurred on the date stated above at 6 a.m.

cause of death: coronary thrombosis

signed: Byron Gillespie, M.D.; Jan. 9, 1935; Woodsfield, O.

 

Submitter's Note:

* These notations were difficult to read and may not be correct.
