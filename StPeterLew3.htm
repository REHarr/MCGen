ST. PETER'S GERMAN EVANGELICAL PROTESTANT CHURCH AT LEWISVILLE, OHIO

 

 

View 3 - Cemetery and back of St. Peter's in Lewisville, Ohio

 

This photo of the St. Peter's German Evangelical Protestant Church of Lewisville, Ohio <<<<<<< HEAD was taken in November 2002.  The cemetery immediately behind the church (shown here) is much older and about half the size of the cemetery behind the photographer.  In this portion of the cemetery, most of the monuments are written in German.  Most have eroded and many are no longer readable.  The inscriptions on most of the monuments in the newer part of the cemetery (behind the photographer) are written in English.  In September of 1962 St. Peters of Lewisville consolidated with two other churches (St. Peter's of Miltonsburg and St. John's, also called Middle Church) to form the Trinity United Church of Christ.  The Trinity United Church of Christ now maintains the cemetery at St. Peter's.
