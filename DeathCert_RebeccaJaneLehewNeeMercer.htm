From the Death Certificate of Rebecca Jane Lehew [nee Mercer]

Green District # 5251, Wetzel Co., WV # 14239

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

residence: Porters Falls, W.Va.

fem., white, widow of Wm. Lehew

birth: Dec. 25, 1858, Tyler Co., W.Va.

age: 75 years 9 months 9 days

trade/profession: housework, own home

father: Jerry Mercer born W.Va.

mother: Alicinda Lotie, born W.Va.

informant: Mrs. Burl Leek, Porters Falls, W.Va.

burial: Mercer Cem., Oct. 6, 1934

undertaker: W.E. Wharton, New Martinsville

MEDICAL CERTIFICATION

date of death: Oct. 4, 1934

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Aug. 10, 1934, to Oct 4, 1934, I last saw her alive on Aug. 10, 1934, death is said to have occurred on the date state above at 10:10 a.m.

principal cause of death: carcinoma (face); onset 1932

contributory: myocarditis & decompensation; onset June 1934

operation: none

test confiming diagnosis: physical ex.

autopsy: no

signature: L. Phelps Stanley, M.D., Pine Grove
