Eddy Cemetery

 

 

This family burial ground is located on the Lloyd Henthorn property on Dogskin Road (County Road 11), Section 34 in Perry Township. Mr. Henthorn believed it was called Eddy Cemetery. There are approximately 15 graves here.

 

Recorded by Francis Mozena on 25 November 1968.

 

Sarah wife of Elias Barker, b 2 May 1817 d 19 Feb 1882, aged 64y 9m 17d

(on same stone) Elias Barker b Dec. 25, 1811 d Jan 4, 1892, aged 80y 10d

 

John Kent d June 28, 1851 in his 54th yr

 

In memory of Mary Marical, d Dec. ninth 1848.
