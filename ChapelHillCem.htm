Chapel Hill Catholic Church Cemetery

Seneca Twp, Monroe County, Ohio

Cemetery inventoried and charted July 6, 2006

By R. E. Harrington

 

 

Latitude and Longitude of Chapel Hill Cemetery: This measurement was made on top of the gravestone of John Adam Ulrich that, as of February 26, 2004, was a stone nearest to the entrance to the cemetery. The Chapel Hill Cemetery can be found by starting at the intersection of Ohio Route # 78 and Monroe County Route # 145 in Lewisville; continue west 3.5 miles on Ohio Route # 78 to a dirt road, Monroe County Route # 33, on the right just beyond the St. Joseph Catholic Church. The church is also on the right or north side of Route # 78. Take Monroe County Route # 33 for 0.7 miles to the first dirt road on the left. This road is marked # 63. Take road # 63 for one mile to the Chapel Hill Cemetery.

Grave rows are approximately 10 feet apart

 

Latitude/Longitude/elevation measurements for the Chapel Hill Cemetery were made on the gravestone of John Adam Ulrich, gravestone # 3 in the chart above. As of February 26, 2004, John Adam Ulrich stone was among the nearest gravestone to the entrance to the cemetery. Accuracy = +/- 17 feet; elevation corrected to ground level = 1,230 feet; Latitude: N 39 o 48.525 Longitude: W081o 15.510

 

The approximate width of the cemetery is 140 feet (measured from the south-most grave stones at the south or left side looking from the entrance, to the north-most grave stone at the north or right side. Left and right are defined as viewed from the entrance of the cemetery). The approximate depth of the cemetery (measured from the 1st row of grave stones, nearest the entrance, to the back fence) is 200 feet. An estimated 40% of the cemetery area has been used. The available area for future graves is located largely at the front, north or right side, and back of the cemetery.
