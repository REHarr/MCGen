       Fred and Georgia celebrated their golden wedding anniversary in 1960.  They had retired from farming and were living in Brooks.  They had an open house in the Brooks hall.

        Fred always enjoyed the farm.  He helped in the garden and grew a large orchard.  Many can remember getting peaches, apricots and apples there.  He loved to go to the timber and on a muddy day you could see him out picking wild raspberries, gooseberries, and etc.

       Several from the community would get together and butcher in the winter.  They would stuff their own sausage, make their own lard, and fry down meat to keep.  They also smoked their hams and kept them in oats.
