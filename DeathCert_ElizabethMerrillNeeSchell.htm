From the Death Certificate for Mrs. Elizabeth Merrill nee Schell

#67009 Belmont Co., OH

Personal: female, white, widowed

Birth: Nov. 24, 1845, Ohio

Age: 74 yrs. _ mos. 23 ds.

Occupation: House Lady

Father: Geo Shell, O.

Mother: Margaret Thomas, O.

Informant: Mrs. ____ Debow, Barnesville, O.

Death: Dec. 17, 1919

"I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from 12/16, 1919, to 12/17, 1919, that I last saw her alive on 12/17, 1919, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 2:30 p.m."

Cause: valvular heart disease; duration 1 year

Contributory: asthma; duration 2 years

Signed: H. J. Arnold, M.D., 12/18,1919, Barnesville O

Burial: Southern Cemetery, 12/20, 1919

Undertaker: W. E. Emerson, Barnesville O.
