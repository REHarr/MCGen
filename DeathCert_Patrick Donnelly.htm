from the Death Certificate for Patrick Donnelly, #556346

place of death: 1845 Lincoln Ave., San Francisco, California

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married

birth:  Sept. 8th, 1840 in Ireland

age:  72 yrs., 3 mos., 28 da.

occupation:  merchant - notions store

father:  ?  born Ireland

mother:  ?  born ?

length of residence

        at place of death:  12 years

        in California:  19 years

informant:  Katherine Donnelly, 1845 Lincoln Ave.

filed:  Jan. 9, 1913; R.G. Brodrick, Registrar or Deputy

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Jan. 6th, 1913

I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Nov. 7, 1912 to Jan. 6, 1913, that I last saw him alive on Jan. 6, 1913, and that death occurred on the date stated above at 3 p.m.

cause of death:  carcinoma of the stomach

duration:  several*

signed: E.M. Weiss, M.D.; Jan. 6, 1913; address:  S.F.

burial:  Cypress Lawn Cemetery; Jan 10, 1913

 

Submitter's Notes:

       *The word SEVERAL was written above where it said YRS. and MOS. so I do not know which it meant.
