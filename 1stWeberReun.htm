First Weber Reunion – the Extended Weber Family

This was the first Weber Family Reunion held in the autumn of 1892.  The reunion is believed to have been held on the farm of Charles Christian (John) Weber.  This farm later became known as the Otto Weber farm.  The participants were as follows:
