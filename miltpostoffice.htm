MILTONSBURG  POST  OFFICE

 

Source: The Spirit of Democracy Thursday, December 10, 1942

 

“The Post Office at Miltonsburg has been discontinued. The recent postmaster retired and no Miltonsburg resident desired to accept the position. Patrons will be served from Woodsfield, Route 3.”
