Obituaries from Lena’s Family History Books

 

Lena Louisa Kahrig Gatten Pettit was a diligent researcher and author.  Her seven family history books contain a wealth of data and information about her extended family lines.  As you might expect her extended families include a large number of the surnames found in Monroe County, Ohio.  Among the information contained in her books are 192 obituaries.  Betty Matson has extracted and retyped the obituaries found in Lena’s books.  Following is an alphabetical index of these obituaries.  The typed obituaries have been incorporated into the “Obituary” section of this website.  [They are also accessible from the Monroe County Historical Society and Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society websites which are linked to this website.]

 

To access individual obituaries listed below, first note the year that follows the name of the person.  Then, go to the “Obituary” section of this website by either clicking on the following address or copying it and pasting it to the address box of your computer browser.  If you are connected to the Internet, you should be able to go directly to the “Obituary” section by clicking on:  http://freepages.history.rootsweb.com/~harringtonfamilies/ObitMasterIndex2.htm.  Then, click on the year following the name of the obituary you are seeking.  This will take you to a page of all available obituaries for that year.  Use the “find” feature of your browser to locate the name you are seeking.  You can also use the index of the “Obituary” section of this website to locate the obituary you are seeking.

 

The letters and number following each name below can be read as follows:  LKP#2, for example, means Lean Kahrig Pettit book number 2.  The number of the book corresponds to the number of the title in the list of Lena’s books and materials found at:  http://freepages.history.rootsweb.com/~harringtonfamilies/LenaKahrigPettit.htm
