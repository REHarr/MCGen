Biographical Sketch of Marjorie Dale Showalter

 

Marjorie Dale Showalter nee Dougherty

Source:  MONROE COUNTY DOUGHERTY CLAN, by James McKitrick, 1989,  p 173

          Marjorie Dale Dougherty was born on 18 October 1926 at Parkersburg, West Virginia.  'Margie' was educated in the Parkersburg School system graduating from Parkersburg High School.  Margie married

Ira Huber Showalter on 11 July 1946.  Margie was a homemaker, never working outside of the home.  Hubert has been a minister for the Church of Christ for many years.  His college years were spent at Freed-Hardeman College at Henderson, Tennessee.  Hubert is now serving the Worthington, Ohio congregation.
