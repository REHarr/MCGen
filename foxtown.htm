Foxtown, Ohio -- about 1910

This is a picture of the store at Foxtown, Ohio.  Foxtown is on County Road 10 near Benwood.  County Road 10 runs from Sardis to SR 255 at Benwood.  Foxtown was never a town, it was just a place in the road.  The store was converted into a house but it burned down about 10 years ago.

 

The only people identified in the picture are second from left, Charlie Yost the owner of the store, the woman sitting by the door is Evie Jones Yost and her two children.  The sign above the door reads “Dr. A. C. Daniels Horse and Dog Medicines.”  This photo was taken about 1910. 

Photograph provided by Karen Romick
