From the Death Certificate for Ida Cline

#70833, Washington Twp., Monroe Co., OH

 

Married

Birth:  Dec. 18, 1899, Ohio

Age:  20 years 7 days

Housewife

Daughter of Friend Dougherty and Louisa Nalley, both born Ohio

Informant:  Fred Cline,* Graysville, Ohio

Date of death:  Dec. 25, 1919

Cause of death:  Ludwigs angina

Contributory cause:  Delatation of ..............[can't read this]

Certifying physician:  R. H. Latta,** Graysville

Burial:  Low Gap Cemetery, Dec. 28, 1919

 

Notes:

*The informant, Fred Cline, could be her young husband Clarence "Frederick" Cline.

** The physician, Reginald Heber Latta, was the grandson of John C. Latta who was the brother of her grandmother Sarah Jane Nalley.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
