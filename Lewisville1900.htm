Lewisville, Ohio - 1900

 

 

This photo of Lewisville, Ohio was taken in 1900.  The orientation for the picture is -- as you enter Lewisville, Ohio from the east on Ohio Route 78, the photographer stood on a small hill on the right.  The picture looks to the south with the right side of the picture being west.  Click to view a 1980 photo of Lewisville, Ohio taken in about the same location as this picture.
