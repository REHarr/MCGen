Ruth Margaret Phinney, daughter of Edith Mabel Harrington

 

Kathleen Joyce Phinney s branch is the most complete example of multi-generational branches of our Harrington family that we found in Nova Scotia. Through the gracious assistance of all the family members we were able to understand and picture the Charles William Harrington s extended family. Carol (Chickie) Hiltz, daughter of Edith Mabel Harrington Phinney s daughter, Ruth Margaret Phinney and Hugh Barry Hiltz, was one of those people. We were fortunate to have met Carol early in our visit and spend considerable time with her. Her father gave her the nick-name Chickie when she was about age 5-years old and, as she says, it stuck. The photographs below add to our understanding of the large clan of Harrington family members that have issued from the immigrant ancestor, Thomas Harrington and his son, Charles William Harrington.
