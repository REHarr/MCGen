Early Marriage Records of Monroe County, Ohio

When the Monroe County courthouse burned in 1867, over 50 years of marriage records were lost. For the past several years, Ruth Hogue has worked diligently on a project to reconstruct a database of some of the marriages that occurred in Monroe County during that time period. Ruth has gleaned information from newspapers, obituaries, family Bibles, and family histories. Realizing this project can never be fully complete, we have decided to publish Ruth’s current database. We are now compiling these records into a book and hope to publish it in the latter part of 200?. If you have marriage records you can contribute, please do so. We will still be adding to the book over the next few months. We are looking specifically for marriages that occurred in Monroe County during the years before 1867. We’ll keep you updated on our progress.
