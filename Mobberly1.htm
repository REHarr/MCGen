Forrest B. and Mary Margaret Dillon Mobberly

This photo of Forrest B. Mobberly and his wife, Mary Margaret Dillon Mobberly was probably taken about August 1915.  In that period, this was the orientation of the typical wedding picture – the groom seated with the bride standing.  Their wedding date was August 18, 1915.  Forrest B. Mobberly was the son of Elias Milton Mobberly and Julia Anne Mann Mobberly.  He was a teacher and is pictured in several photos of classes of the Way School on this web site.  These can be viewed by clicking on the selection highlighted below.  Forrest B. Mobberly and his wife met an untimely death on April 3, 1940 in an automobile accident.  Details of this accident is given in their obituary which is also included in the list of links at the end of this paragraph.
