TOBACCO ON THE WAY TO MARKET

 

 

Every now and then there is a picture that speaks so loudly of life in the days of our early Monroe County ancestors -- the picture worth a thousand words.  This is one such picture.  Written on the back of this picture are these words: "They always said this was taken in Miltonsburg."

If it were taken in Miltonsburg, the house in the background belonged to Fred Stalder (Lot # 6). The main street of the town is just to the right. (Click here to see the plan of "Plan of Miltonsburg.")  The people in the photo would be looking toward Yunkes' Store on Lot# 5. The wagon, which is loaded with two hogsheads of tobacco, is headed east.  There were several tobacco houses in Miltonsburg at various times.
