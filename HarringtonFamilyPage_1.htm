Clan in Pickaway County, Ohio

 

 

 

Sarah Jane Valentine (1848-1933) was born 28 June 1848 to Levi J. and Mary Bolinger Valentine on a farm near Circleville, Ohio, USA. She was the oldest daughter of a pioneer family that, over the next nearly 2 decades, moved westward to four different locations and finally arriving in Kansas Territory. It was in Kansas near Fort Leavenworth that Sarah Jane met Charles William Harrington, a seaman from Nova Scotia, Canada who had arrived on a cattle boat, probably to buy cattle to transport for a client. Sarah Jane became pregnant with Charles Harrington. It is believed that they did not marry.
