From the Death Certificate for William Franklin Weber

Monroe County, Ohio, Summit Township, Lewisville, # 20401

 

Residence: 49 years, Lewisville, Ohio

Male, white, married to Sarah Weber

Birth: Feb. 2, 1888, Monroe Co., Ohio

Age: 49 years 1 month 11 days

Occupation: oil field worker; driller

Parents: Christian Weber and Savilla Keylor, both of Monroe Co., Ohio

Informant: Phyllis I. Weber* of Lewisville, Ohio

Burial: Lewisville Cemetery, Mar. 16, 1937

Date of death: Mar. 13, 1937, 8 a.m.

Cause of death: Hung himself in his own barn. Neck was broken by jumping from hay loft. Had rope around neck.

Certified by: Paul Turner, coroner, address Woodsfield, Ohio, 3/13/1937

 

Note: *The informant was his daughter Phyllis Irene Weber.
