FORAKER  COVERED  BRIDGE

 

 

Foraker Covered Bridge -- 1964

 

This photo of the Foraker Covered Bridge was taken in 1964.  It is located in Monroe County, 3 miles east of Graysville, Ohio.  It spanned the Little Muskingum River.

 

Photograph provided by Lena Kahrig Pettit
