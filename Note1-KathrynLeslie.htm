E-mail from Kathryn Leslie dated April 11, 2006: Elizabeth was first married to Joseph Hines.  She was born in NY.  He was born in VA.  THEIR daughter was Amy, not a daughter of Benjamin McVay.  Joseph had died by the 1820 census.  Elizabeth Hines was in Seneca, Monroe OH, with 2 sons under 10 (I think Joseph and Thomas) 2 females 10-16 (don't know), 1 daughter under 10 (my AMY) 2 females 16-26 and Elizabeth 26-45.  Earliest birth date for Elizabeth 1775.  Disparity between the 1850 and 1860 census for her age later.  She married Benj. McVey.  I just found about the re-marriage a few days ago.  That explains why I couldn't find Elizabeth or Joseph. 

 

The Hill/McVay/Dearth/Henthorn/ etc. names kept coming up.  Quite a few of them moved to WI where my Amy went.
