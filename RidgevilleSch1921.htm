RIDGEVILLE SCHOOL

Ridgeville School was located 6 mi. on Co. Rd. 10 (Benwood Rd.) at the intersection of Twp Rd. 480, Lengacher Rd. The school closed in 1942.

Some of the teachers who taught there are Velma Reef, Charlie Straight, Banche Tubaugh, Wilma Zoff, Mildred Wright, Harold Turner, Clarence Reef, I.M.Straight, Elizabeth Hinderlong, Raymond Hollingsworth, Elizabeth Bethel, Ruby Price, Marie Thompson.
