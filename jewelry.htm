New Jewelry Store

Published in The Spirit of Democracy dated Thursday, September 18, 1924


 “H. A. Leibfarth, formerly with H. L. Bott of Woodsfield, but for several months past employed at Barnesville, will open a jewelry and repair shop in room recently prepared for him on the Public Square between Armstrong’s Department Store and Armstrong & Kerr’s clothing store. Mr. Leibfarth is a Monroe County man, has had considerable experience, and will no doubt succeed well in his new enterprise. This new establishment will probably be open to the public next week.”
