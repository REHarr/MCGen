From the Death Certificate for Fred Stalder

Miltonsburg, Monroe County, Ohio #66413

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married

birth: Sept. 24" 1856;* Sardis, Ohio

age: 80 years* 25 days

trade: retired laborer

father: Nicolis Stalder, born Switzerland

mother: Don't know, born Switzerland

informant: Freda Ankrom; Miltonsburg, Ohio

burial: Miltonsburg, O.; Oct. 21" 1937*

funeral director: L.J. Bauer; Woodsfield, O.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: 10-19, 1937

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Oct 12, 1937 to Oct 18, 1937, I last saw him alive on Oct 18, 1937, death is said to have occurred on the date stated above at 6.00 a.m.

principal cause of death: arterio sclerosis & myrlol** cardeles**

signature: O.C. Jackson, M.D.; 10-20, 1937; Woodsfield, Ohio

 

Submitter's Notes:

* If he were 80 years old and died in 1937, his birth would have been in 1857, not 1856.

** This was difficult to read and cannot be correct as typed above.
