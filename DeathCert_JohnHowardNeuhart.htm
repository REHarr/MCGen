From the Death Certificate for John Howard Neuhart*

 

Barnesville, Belmont County, Ohio #63914

residence: 719 Wiley Ave.

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, single

birth: June 1, 1924; Barnesville, Ohio

age: 6 months 20 days

father: Ed. Neuhart, born Louisville [sic], Ohio

mother: Lilly Thornberry, born Somerton, Ohio

informant: Mr. Ed. Neuhart;* Barnesville, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: 12/21/1924

physician's statement: I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from 11/25, 1924, to 12/21, 1924, that I last saw him alive on 12/20, 1924, and that death

occurred, on the date stated above at 10:30 a.m.

cause of death: uremic poisoning; duration, 20 days

contributory: bronchial pneumonia; duration, 28 days

signature. D. O. Sheppard, M.D.; Barnesville, O

burial: Northern Cemetery, Barnesville, O.; 12/23/1924

undertaker: John A. Campbell; Barnesville, Ohio

 

*Submitter s note: On this death certificate the last name is spelled NEUHART both at the top of the form and at the informant s signature. However, on another child of the same couple that died, Dona Jeanne, the name at the top is NEUHARDT and the signature, in a totally different handwriting, is MR. EDMUND NEUHARDT.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
