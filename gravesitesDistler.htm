Free Pictures to Selected Gravesites

 

Looking for an ancestor s grave? This might help. Dave Distler has been doing historical and genealogical research on his family lines in Monroe County, Ohio. He recently announced his project to make photos of gravesites available to other researchers. These data are available free as described by Dave below. Dave also has a pay-for-service website at: http://orvf.com.

 

Dave Distler writes:

 

I have been photographing hundreds of headstones and updating my orvf.com database with selected interments in Summit Township cemeteries over the past couple of years.

 

My main listing webpage is on a free server called findagrave.com and can be found here:

 

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=mr&MRid=46528272

 

Surnames can be searched from the above link.

 

Of specific interest to those researching Monroe County would be:

 

Middle (St John's) Church Cemetery in Summit Township:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=43122

 

Evangelical Reformed Church Cemetery in Lewisville:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=1182423

 

Friendship Cemetery in Lewisville:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=40970

 

Miltonsburg (St Peters) Church Cemetery in Malaga Township:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=42160

 

Buchanan Presbyterian Church Cemetery in Summit Township:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=1183424

 

Bracken Ridge Church Cemetery in Summit Township:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=1160518

 

Oaklawn Cemetery in Woodsfield:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gsr&GScid=42492

 

Pioneer Cemetery in Woodsfield, Ohio:

http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=cr&GRid=12656226&CRid=42686

 

Feel free to link to it from your web pages.  You may use this text to create a new html page if you wish.

 

Visitors to individuals in findagrave can add additional photos of the headstone or the individual or simply leave digital flowers as a reminder of their visit.

 

There may also be individuals in these cemeteries that I have not listed.  The only ones I have listed are those already in my database.  New listings can be added by anyone registering at findagrave for free and adding their listings.  Be sure the individual is not already shown.

 

I hope sharing this information will be of help to you and others.  My main database contains only allied families and lineage-linked individuals, either of myself or my wife.

 

My direct Monroe County lines include the surnames: DISTLER, MORRIS, JUNG (YOUNG), LEISURE, VAN FOSSEN and SWAIN.

 

While not 100% documented, my direct line is to William MORRIS b. 1782 Greene Co., PA d. 1843 in Monroe Co., OH. 

 

Dave Distler

Ohio River Valley Families

http://orvf.com
