PurpleHeart Mansel E. Flood

 

Mancel Flood

Source: Quaker City Home Towner [Quaker City, Ohio]; Friday, 9 Feb. 1945

Mrs. Mancel Flood has received the Purple Heart from her husband, T/Sgt. Mancel Flood. He was wounded in France Nov. 18, 1944. He entered the armed services Aug. 11th, 1942, with the 377th Infantry. T/Sgt. Flood had training in Texas, Louisianna, California and Pennsylvanian and went overseas in August 1944. He is now in a U.S. hospital in England. His wife, the former Jennie Leach of Summerfield and his two little children, Beverly Ann and Ronnie Lee, reside in Batesville.
