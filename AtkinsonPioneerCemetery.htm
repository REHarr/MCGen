Atkinson-Pioneer Cemetery

Salem Township, Monroe County, Ohio

 

 

>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>

 

This old Cemetery is located on OLD RT 78. in Clarington.   As you are headed EAST on Rt. 78, going into Clarington, OLD RT. 78  turns off the present Rt. 78 to the left shortly after entering the city limits.  Atkinson-Pioneer Cemetery is just a short distance off RT 78 on the left above the street.
