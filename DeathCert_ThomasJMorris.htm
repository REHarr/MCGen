From the Death Certificate for Thomas J. Morris

 

The certificate of death, #23778, Malaga Township, Monroe County, Ohio, says that Thomas J. Morris was born in Woodsfield, Ohio, July 13, 1845, and lived 78 years 8 months and 28 days. The informant was Alva Morris [his son] who did not know the birthplaces of Thomas J.'s parents--Henry Morris and Nancy* Kinkade. The cause of death on April 10, 1924, was gastro enterio catarrh according to Dr. G. W. ( ? ) of Jerusalem.  The disease was contracted "at home."  He was buried April 13 in a private cemetery .*

 

*Submitter's Notes:  Thomas J. Morris' mother was MARY Kinkade.  The private cemetery is located on the Morris homestead off  Miltonsburg-Calais Road in Woodsfield, Ohio.  He was the last one known to be buried in this cemetery.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
