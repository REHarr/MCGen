Obituary of Samuel Seymore Latta (b. 2 Jun 1833; d. 7 Jun 1912)

 

Source:  The Spirit of Democracy: 

 

"Samuel Latta, 80 y, died at home in Wayne Tp., 8 June 1912.  Four children and wife survive.  He was Civil War Veteran, buried Moose Ridge Cemetery."
