From the Death Certificate for Jacob Neuhart

Barnesville, Belmont Co., Ohio, #515

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married

birth:  8-6-1849--Ohio

age:  66 yrs. 5 mos. 5 da.

occupation:  contractor

father:  Phillip Neuhart, born Germany

mother:  Mary Schaub, born Germany

informant:  Emma F. DeNoon:* Pa.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Jan 31, 1916

physician's statement:  I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Jan 20, 1916, to Jan 31, 1916, that I last saw him alive on Jan 31, 1916, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 7 p.m.

cause of death:  Broncho Pneumonia; duration, 10 da.

contributory:  Organic Heart

signature:  J. A. Juakens,** M.D., 2-2-1916; Barnesville, O.

burial:  Southern Cemetery; 2-3-1916

undertaker:  M.S. Clelland & Kennard;** City

 

Submitter's Notes:

       *Emma F. DeNoon was the younger sister of the Charlotte Denoon who married Jacob Neuhart.

        ** The handwriting of these names is difficult to read and may be in error.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
