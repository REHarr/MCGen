From the Death Certificate for Howard A.*   Turner

Woodsfield, Monroe County, Ohio #29982

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, married to Lillie Turner

birth:  July 23, 1904; Monroe Co., O.

age:  24 years, 8 months, 15 days

occupation:  laborer in oil field

father:  Frank Turner, born Monroe Co., O.

mother:  Anna E. Latta, born Monroe Co., O.

informant:  Mrs. A. E. Turner; Woodsfield, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  Apr. 8, 1929

physician's statement:  I hereby certify that I attended deceased from Feb. 8, 1929       to April 4, 1929, that I  last saw him alive on April 4, 1929, and that death       occurred, on the date stated above, at 3:30 a.m. cause of death:  colitis dysenterie, in character probably amebic

duration:  1 yrs.

where disease contracted:  Texas?

operation:  no

autopsy:  no

signed:  H.P. Gillespie,** M.D.; April 8, 1929; Woodsfield, O.

burial:  Oak Lawn Cem.; 4/10/1929

undertaker:  W. P. Galbraith; Woodsfield

 

Submitter's Notes: 

       * On the Monroe County, Ohio, Marriage CD 11, page 104, his name was listed as HOWARD O. TURNER.

     ** This writing was difficult to read and may be in error.
