The identity of this group is not known. On the back of this picture is written, "Monroe Co. Students at Scio 1902 - Spring Time." The owner of this picture believes that these may be students of the Monroe County Normal School, a teachers college that was operated in Woodsfield, Ohio for many years. Any information about the group, individuals in the picture, or the location would be greatly appreciated. Please send any information to Tim Morton and/or Dick Harrington, webmaster.
