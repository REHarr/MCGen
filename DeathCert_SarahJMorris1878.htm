From the Death Certificate of Sarah J. Morris

West Virginia State Department of Health #12181

PLACE OF DEATH: Cowen, Glade District, Webster County, West Virginia

PERSONAL

residence: Arcola, Webster Co., W. Va.

female, white, widowed from John J. Morris

birth: May 10, 1878; Webster Co., W. Va.

age at death: 69 years 4 months 6 days

occupation: housewife

father: Hesekahr Green (deceased), born Webster Co., W. Va.

mother: Mary Carpenter (deceased), born Webster Co., W. Va.

informant: Ben Morris, Cowen, W. Va.

burial: Sept. 28, 1947, Arcola Cemetery, Arcola, W. Va.

funeral director: C.E. Dodd, Webster Springs, W. Va.

MEDICAL CERTIFICATION

date of death: Sept. 26, 1947, at 1:30 p.m.

physician's statement: I certify that death occurred on the date above stated; that I attended deceased from 1939 to Sept 26, 1947, and that I last saw her alive on Sept 26, 1947.

immediate cause of death: myocardial failure

due to: paroximal tachycardia; duration 10 years

other conditions: arterio-sclerosis, hypertension

signature: ______________*, coroner, Sept 30, 1947
