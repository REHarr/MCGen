John WILKINSON was born about 1764 in New Jersey. He died in May 1833 in Belmont County, Ohio. John Cunningham III in The Descendants of Amos C. Reed and Jemima Kennard named John's children as: Joel, John, Israel (b 1802), Moses, Thomas, Phoebe, William and Israel. I cannot locate information on Moses Wilkinson in Belmont County, but there is a marriage record for a Phoebe Wilkinson. The following land deeds identify the heirs of John Wilkinson.  Moses and Phoebe are not listed.

 

Belmont County, Ohio - Volume R Page 352 - 18 Apr 1834, Amy Wilkinson, widow of John, Joel and Jane, John and Sarah, Thomas and Mary, William and Nancy (the signature states Ann J.) Israel and Mary, Jesse and Polly, Isaac Merrit and Keturah, James George and Rachel, James Glovier and Edith, Lewis Foreman and Hannah, Isaac N. Becket and Nancy, William Greenlee and Hopy, Amy Wilkinson Junior, Ruth Wilkinson and Bathsheba Wilkinson to William H. Manly of Belmont County. SW 1/4 of S31, R4, T7, surveyed by John Stewart from George Snider to John Wilkinson, dec., and to the deed of conveyance from said Snider to said Wilkinson dated June 24, 1808, and recorded in Book B pg 449.

 

The birth dates of Nancy, Ruth and Bathsheba appear to eliminate them as children of John and Ama. They must be grandchildren, possibly of a son who was deceased by this date (1834).
