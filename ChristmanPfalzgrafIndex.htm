Philip Christman-Rosina Pfalsgraf

by Lena Kahrig Gatten

 

Lenas family tree book of her Christman-Pfalzgraf ancestors is over 250 pages.  The webspace required to accommodate the photographs of the 250-plus pages is too large to include on this website.  The full text of the book has been made into a CD and is available at http://freepages.history.rootsweb.com/~harringtonfamilies/CD-Status.htm.
