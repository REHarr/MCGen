From the Death Certificate for Cole Leach:

 

The certificate of death #5354 Monroe County, Ohio 1941, if for Cole Leach. He was born in Calais, Ohio, Mar. 30 [no year given], the son of Ace Leach and Harriet Rodes [submitter’s note: this is an error, see below], birthplaces of parents unknown.  He was married to Laney and was a farmer.  He died in Seneca Twp. of Monroe County, Ohio, on Aug. 28, 1941, having lived 63 years 4 months and 28 days. The informant was Rufus Leach. [He had a brother named Rufus.] Dr. John F. Like [?difficult to read] attributed his cause of death as pellagra arteriosclerosis.  He was buried in the Calais Cemetery on Aug. 30, 1941.

 

Submitter’s note:  One curious thing on this certificate is that although his father is listed as Ace Leach, his mother's maiden name is listed as Harriet Rodes, instead of Harriet Leek as on other records.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
