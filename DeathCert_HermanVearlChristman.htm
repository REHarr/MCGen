From the death certificate of Herman Vearl Christman

Seneca Township, Monroe Co., OH, #79606

 

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, infant

birth: December 3, 1917, Monroe Co., Ohio

age: 26 days

father: Philip Franklin Christman born Ohio

mother: Cora Gutberlet* born Ohio

informant: P.F. Christman, Woodsfield R 3

filed: 12-30-1917; W. N. Weber [or possibly Wehr), Registrar*

 

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death: December 29, 1917

I hereby certify, that I attended deceased from Dec. 3, 1917, to Dec 29, 1917, that I last saw him alive on Dec. 25, 1917, and that death occurred, on the date stated above at 9 a.m.

cause: premature birth; congenital debility; duration: 26 days

signed: W. P. Johnson, M.D., Dec. 29, 1917, Miltonsburg, O

burial: Seneca Twp Cemetery,** 12 ___*** 1917

 

Submitter's Notes:

* Writing difficult to read so not positive of spelling.

** Herman Vearl and his twin brother are in the Calais Cemetery, Calais, Monroe Co., OH.

*** No day was given for the burial.

 

Provided by Betty Latta Kitchen
