From the Death Certificate for Susannah Morris:

 

Susannah Morris

Death certificate #18644 says Susannah Morris was the widow of Thomas Morris, born March 27th, 1853; lived 80 years, 11 months, and 12 days. She did houshold duties. She was the d/o John Markle and Sarah Folger. The informant, A. M. Morris, [she had a son Alva McKinley Morris] of Malaga, Rt. 1, Ohio, did not know their birthplaces. She died March 15, 1934 in Malaga Township of Monroe County, Ohio. Dr. H. Gillespie said the cause of death was myocarditis. She was buried in Calais on March 17.
