From the death certificate of Wilina Kinkade,* #62142

Bethel Township, Monroe County, Ohio

female, white

birth: Mar. 5, 1914

age: 7 mos 9 da

father Douglas Kinkade, born Ohio; mother Ada Nalley, born Ohio

informant: Douglas Kinkade,* * Graysville . . . .

death: Nov. 14, 1914, 4 p.m.* * *

attended by physician from Oct. 1, 1914 to Nov. 10, 1914 when last seen alive by physician

cause of death: tuberculosis

physician: J. S. Peter,* * * * Harriettsville

burial: Pleasant R. Cem., Nov. 15, 1914

 

Submitter's Notes:

* The cursive writing of her name on this certificate appears to be Wilina to me. In the book by Ben Dixon, OUR BOOK: OUR ANCESTOR, OURSELVES, OUR CHILDREN it is listed as Willene. In the MONROE COUNTY, OHIO CEMETERY INSCRIPTIONS: BENTON AND WASHINGTON TOWNSHIPS book published by the Monroe County Chapter of the Ohio Genealogical Society her tombstone shows Neva W. as the d/o D.J. & A. H. Kinkade. The transcriber that listed the information on the Ohio Death Index that was used on line (<http://www.ohiohistory.org/dindex/index.cfm>) to get the certificate number gave her name as Thelma.
