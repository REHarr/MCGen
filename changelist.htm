FOR OUR FREQUENT AND NOT-SO-FREQUENT VISITORS -- Everyone is Busy!! - right? We realize that you may not have the time or interest to revisit every nook and cranny of the web site just to find out if anything has been added or changed. Yet, you are pretty sure that the site does get changed often and you do not want to miss anything. After all, the MCC of OGS web site IS a major source of genealogical information for those interested in Monroe County ancestors and descendants. And, that is probably why you are visiting in the first place.

Well, here is something that hopefully will save you time and also assure that you do not miss anything of interest or importance.

Below is a list of each significant addition or change to the web site. These modifications are listed with the dates that they were made. Also, a hyperlink is included so that you can quickly go to the addition or change without having to try to remember where it is on the site.

Note: Surnames and Queries are added to the site on an almost daily basis. Notification of the addition of Surnames and Queries are not included on in this list of modifications. Please consult the Surnames and Queries list for new additions. They are arranged alphabetically on the S & Q list.

Please let us know what you think of this service -- use the convenient Guest Book to register your comments.
