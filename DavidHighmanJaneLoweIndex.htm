David Highman & Jane Lowe 1800-1979

by Lena Kahrig Gatten

 

Lena’s family tree book of her “David Highman & Jane Lowe” ancestors has nearly 130 pages.  The webspace required to accommodate the photographs of the 130 pages is too large to include on this website.  The full text of the book has been made into a CD and is available at: http://freepages.history.rootsweb.com/~harringtonfamilies/CD-Status.htm.
