From the Death Certificate for Joseph Hartford Christy

Woodsfield, Center Township, Monroe County, Ohio #31692

PERSONAL AND STATISTICAL PARTICULARS

male, white, single

birth:  May 19, 1872, Woodsfield, Ohio

age at death:  69 years 10 months 14 days

occupation:  retired housepainter

father:  name (don't know); location (don't know)

mother:  Anna Pool, born Woodsfield, Ohio

informant:  Asher O Paulus, Woodsfield, Ohio

burial:  Woodsfield, May 5, 1942

funeral firm:  W. P. Galbraith, Woodsfield, Ohio

MEDICAL CERTIFICATE OF DEATH

date of death:  5/3/1942

statement:  I hereby certify that I last saw him dead 5/3/1942; death is said to have         occurred on the date stated above at 2:30p.m.

cause:  Found dead on the porch of his home.  Probably acute coronary occlusion.

contributory causes:  over use of liquor

signed:  Paul R. Turner, Coroner; 5/4/1942; Woodsfield, O.
